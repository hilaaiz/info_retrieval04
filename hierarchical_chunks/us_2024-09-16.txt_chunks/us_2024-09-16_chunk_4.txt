Mr. Scott began his career as a religion teacher at St. Joseph's Collegiate Institute in 1971.
Over time, he served as a social studies teacher, vice principal, principal, and then president.
In 2001, Mr. Scott earned his Letters of Affiliation, the highest honor bestowed by the Institute of Brothers of Christian Schools, the religious order that founded St. Joseph's Collegiate Institute in 1861.
