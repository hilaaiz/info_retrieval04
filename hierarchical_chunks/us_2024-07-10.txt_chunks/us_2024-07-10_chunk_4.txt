Beyond his pastoral work, Pastor Gillison is committed to serving his community in all capacities.
Through his leadership with the Concerned Clergy Coalition, the Baptist Ministers Conference of Buffalo & Vicinity, and the Urban Think Tank, Pastor Gillison is a guiding figure in the community.
Further, Pastor Gillison leads the Mount Olive Development Corporation, a non-profit organization founded by his late father, Pastor William Gillison.
With the Mount Olive Development Corporation, Pastor Gillison provides resources for families on Buffalo's East Side to address their economic, social, and educational needs.
Pastor Gillison established the Buffalo Raiders Little League Football team, a safe place for youth to participate in healthy programming.
Today, Pastor Gillison is a loving husband to his wife, Victoria, father of three, and grandfather of three.
He continues to unify our community through his devotion to faith and commitment to service.
Please join me in congratulating Pastor Dwayne R. Gillison, Sr. for receiving the Community Leadership Award and thanking him for his remarkable dedication to our community.
