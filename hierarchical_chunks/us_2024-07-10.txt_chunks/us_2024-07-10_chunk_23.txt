Mr. FALLON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize 
Mrs. JoAnne Soules of Pittsfield, Massachusetts, and present her with the Congressional Patriot Award.
JoAnne has dedicated herself to faithfully serving and uplifting her community.
JoAnne was born in Pittsfield and attended Pittsfield High School.
In 1969, she graduated from Elms College with a bachelor's degree in history.
JoAnne also obtained her master's degree in educational administration and leadership from Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts.
She later taught social studies at South Junior High, now known as Herberg Middle School.
In 1994, JoAnne transferred to Pittsfield High, where she taught history before being appointed to serve as the Dean of Students and Vice Principal.
