I thank the hon.
Gentleman for highlighting the report by StandWithUs UK.
Antisemitism has no place in our lecture halls, campuses and universities, and no place in our society.
Universities must be places where all students feel safe and respected, and are able to thrive.
A week ago today, I brought together university vice-chancellors and community leaders to hear testimonials from Jewish students, and to discuss next steps to ensure safer and more inclusive campuses for all our students.
