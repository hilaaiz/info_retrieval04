Mrs. Bishop-Brown, the eldest of nine children, was born on June 24, 1924 to King Bishop and Julia Nesmith Bishop in Dorsey Town, South Carolina.
Having given her life to the Lord, Mary established her first faith connections while attending St. Mark's African Methodist Episcopal Church as a young girl.
In young adulthood, she took a job working as a cook.
However, Mary's interests quickly expanded beyond the kitchen when she met Miley Brown, the love of her life.
At first sight, the two were immediately smitten and bound for a bountiful future together.
