Several other units have more than two bedrooms to meet the needs of multigenerational households, which has been an option long missing from the local housing landscape.
One of the most notable features of this project is the inclusion of indoor and outdoor play areas for children, facilities that are too often overlooked in affordable housing developments.
Alongside these play spaces are private entrances, accessible layouts, and culturally relevant design elements all chosen to support daily life, connection, and communal care.
