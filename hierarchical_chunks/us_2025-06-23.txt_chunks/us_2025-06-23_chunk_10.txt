Mr. KRISHNAMOORTHI.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor Didi Krishna Kumari, a revered spiritual leader, humanitarian, and educator whose lifelong commitment to service has uplifted communities across the globe.
Born on May 9, 1961, Didi Krishna began her spiritual journey at the age of three under the guidance of saints Sadhu T.L. Vaswani and Dada J.P. Vaswani.
She now serves as the head of the Sadhu Vaswani Mission, leading with humility, wisdom, and dedication to service.
Founded in the 1930's, the Sadhu Vaswani Mission has flourished under Didi Krishna's leadership into a global force for compassion and healing.
With more than 60 centers across five continents, the Mission carries out extensive work in education, health care, spiritual development, and humanitarian relief.
