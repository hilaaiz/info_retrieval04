I welcome the Government’s welfare reforms and celebrate the millions of additional people now in work thanks to this Conservative Government.
I note that every Labour Government there has ever been has left more people unemployed and on the dole queue at the end than at the beginning—theirs is a truly disgraceful record.
However, can my right hon.
Friend assure my constituents who may be chronically ill or vulnerable that, although there will be support in place, they will not be forced back into work if that is not appropriate?
