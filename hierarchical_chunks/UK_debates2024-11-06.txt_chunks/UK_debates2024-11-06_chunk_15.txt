Will the Secretary of State express her regret about the impact of Labour’s Budget on Wales?
It will undoubtedly make the people of Wales poorer.
Pensioners have been left wondering whether they should turn on their heating this winter, and family farms will be destroyed.
The Budget is affecting business confidence and employment across the nation.
The Budget provides the biggest ever settlement in real terms since the dawn of devolution, a protected £80 million for steelworkers and businesses, £88 million for city and growth deals, £320 million for funding through investment zones, £25 million for coal tips and a protected £440 million for local communities— I could go on.
It was a great deal for Wales.
