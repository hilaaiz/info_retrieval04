Mr. HIGGINS.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor Willie Ann Huff, who on July 15, 2023, will celebrate her 90th birthday.
Mrs. Huff is the daughter of the late Sarah and Willie Vinson, and the eldest of 11 children.
Willie Ann Huff has 5 children of her own, Gloria, the late James Vinson, Edward, Timothy, and Debra.
She is also the grandmother of 12, the great-grandmother of 30, and has 4 great-great grandchildren.
