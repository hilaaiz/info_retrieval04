Senator Dunn spent her life giving back to her community.
She served in a leadership capacity on numerous organizations, including President of the Concerned Citizens of Bessemer Cut-Off, Coordinator for the Jefferson County Democratic Conference, Board Member for the Bessemer Civic Center, President of the Humanistic Challengers Federated Club, Secretary of Girls, Inc. of Central Alabama, Board Member of the Jefferson/Shelby Counties American Cancer Society Advisory Board and Board Member for the State Democratic Executive Committee and many more.
Having accepted Christ into her life at a young age, 
Senator Dunn was a devoted servant of God who led by example and always practiced what she preached.
She was an instructor for the Jefferson County Congress home church, the Shady Grove Baptist Church.
