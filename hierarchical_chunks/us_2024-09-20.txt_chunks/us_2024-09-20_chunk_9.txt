Mrs. LESKO.
Mr. Speaker, on behalf of Arizona's 8th Congressional District, I would like to recognize Donna Rhodes, president of the Sun City Grand Republican Club.
Donna has distinguished herself by providing exceptional service to the residents of Sun City Grand, Arizona.
As president of the club, Donna has been an advocate for educating members on the civic engagement process and how to interact with elected officials.
The efforts of Donna and the Sun City Grand Republican Club have been pivotal to the success of elected officials and candidates in the Phoenix West Valley.
I am thankful for Donna's leadership and dedication to conservative principles.
I sincerely appreciate her service to the 8th Congressional District and the State.
