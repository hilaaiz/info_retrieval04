I welcome the record real-terms funding flowing into our schools, but will my right hon.
Friend join me in looking very carefully at the case for extending funding for tutoring?
It has raised attainment, in particular for the most disadvantaged, in many of our schools, and been seen as a great success story.
When it was introduced, it was intended to be a long-term intervention.
May I urge the Minister to continue to look at that and ensure we find money, in addition to the pupil premium, to support that noble aim?
