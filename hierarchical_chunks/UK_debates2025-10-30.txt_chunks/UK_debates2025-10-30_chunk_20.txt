I welcome my hon.
Friend’s support for sectoral collective bargaining and collective agreements as a key steer for improving living standards across workplaces in the country.
As he knows, we are demonstrating our commitment to sectoral collective bargaining with the social care and school support staff sectors.
The UK is committed to working internationally to strengthen workers’ rights and enhance Labour standards globally.
We fully support the work of the International Labour Organisation and will continue to meet our obligations under the ILO.
