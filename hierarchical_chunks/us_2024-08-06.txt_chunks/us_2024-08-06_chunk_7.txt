Mr. Tate was not just a real estate mogul, but a visionary whose successful ventures transformed the real estate landscape, particularly in Miami Beach.
His entrepreneurial spirit continues to inspire us all.
In addition to his real estate career, 
Mr. Tate's business acumen was instrumental during a crucial period when the government needed his skills.
As the Southeast Regional Director of The Resolution Trust Corporation,
a federal agency established to address the Savings and Loans financial crisis, 
Mr. Tate's invaluable contributions helped the United States overcome these financial challenges.
One of 
Mr. Tate's greatest legacies is the creation of the Stanley G. Tate Florida Prepaid College Program.
This program, founded in 1987, has been a beacon of hope for countless students in Florida, providing them with the opportunity to pursue higher education.
Mr. Tate's foresight and dedication to educational equality have been invaluable to the community, and his legacy lives on in the lives of the students who have benefited from his college savings plan.
