Mr. GARAMENDI.
Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, I rise today to recognize the contributions of Jameelah Hanif as she is honored as one of California's 8th Congressional District's Women of the Year.
Jameelah is a community organizer, family advocate, and champion for children.
She is the Founder, CEO, and Executive Director of Watch Me Grow, Inc, a non-profit organization that provides resources and training for parents.
Jameelah facilitates the Parent Leadership Training Institute to help parents advocate for their children and families.
She has been a small business owner for 10 years, providing quality childcare and family services to over 150 children.
Jameelah has also been the Chair of the Solano County Local Child Care Planning Council and the Solano County Licensed Family Child Care Association.
She has a degree in Child Development and Family Services, and completed the Solano County Parent Leadership Training Institute, Leadership Vallejo, and EmergeCA programs.
