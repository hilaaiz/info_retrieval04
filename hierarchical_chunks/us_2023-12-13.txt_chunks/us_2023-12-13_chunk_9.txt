Mr. HOYER.

Mrs. Michelle Y. Cromwell.

Throughout her career, 
Mrs. Cromwell has served the Department of the Army faithfully and personified the Army's values of loyalty, duty, respect, selfless service, honor, integrity, and personal courage.
