Ms. VAN DUYNE.
Mr. Speaker, I rise to honor our Texas-24 Hometown Hero, Mirchelle Louis.
Mirchelle Louis has spent the past 18 years smoothing the cancer journey for everyone who's walked through the doors.
As a clinical social worker, she joined the organization in 2006 providing comprehensive support to cancer patients free of charge.
During her time working at the organization, she briefly served as Executive Director of Susan G. Komen for the Cure North Texas.
