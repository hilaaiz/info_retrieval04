Mr. VAN DREW.
Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, it is my honor and privilege to commemorate the induction of Ralph Contini as the National President of UNICO National.
Ralph, an exceptional leader with fourty years of dedicated service to UNICO at all levels, currently serves as its Executive Vice President and Chairman of the UNICO National Columbus Day Committee.
Throughout his tenure, he has been recognized for his unwavering commitment and received prestigious awards, including the Dr. Anthony P. Vastola Gold Medal Award and the Outstanding District Governor Award.
Born and raised in Italy, Ralph's deep connection to Italian heritage has shaped his remarkable journey.
Beyond UNICO, Ralph actively participates in several Italian American organizations, holding esteemed positions such as Treasurer/Executive Director at the Tri-State Italian American Congress and serving on the Board of Trustees for The Columbians.
His dedication was further recognized with the honorable title of Cavaliere by the President of the Republic of Italy.
As District Governor of New Jersey Districts Six and Seven, Ralph received the ``Outstanding'' and ``Distinguished'' UNICO National District Governor Awards, recognizing his outstanding leadership and dedicated efforts in managing the chapters within each district.
Today, as the proud owner of Ralph A. Contini, CPA, LLC in Paramus, he provides top-tier accounting, tax, consulting, and financial planning services.
Now residing Hackensack with his family, Ralph finds joy and fulfillment in his family life and values the support they provide as he continues to make a meaningful impact to their community.
His legacy as an outstanding leader and advocate Italian heritage stands as a testament to his deep commitment and passion to the cause.
Congratulations to Ralph for his induction as National President at UNICO and I thank him for his dedication to his community.
God Bless Ralph Contini and God Bless the United States.
