Mr. Goodwin was born in a small community near Denver, Colorado, in 1960.
His love of the outdoors and passion for serving others led him to begin his career as a National Park Service law enforcement ranger.
For 27 years, 
Mr. Goodwin worked in National Parks across the continental United States, traveling from the Rocky Mountains in Colorado, to Mount Rainier in Washington State, to the Everglades in Florida, and finally to Golden Gate National Recreation Area in the San Francisco Bay Area.
