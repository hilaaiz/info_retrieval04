Beyond its contributions to defense and state operations, Envision Dallas offers an array of essential services that will benefit countless families in North Texas.
The Cathy G. Hudson Envision Child Development Center will nurture our youngest learners, ensuring children of all abilities have the foundation they need to succeed.
The Perot Welcome Center will serve as a vital resource for families and individuals seeking support.
The newly established Envision Vision and Rehabilitation Clinic will provide cutting-edge care to those with vision impairments, while the William L. Hudson Workforce Innovation Center will empower individuals who are blind or visually impaired to gain the skills and confidence necessary to excel in today's workforce.
Envision Dallas is setting a powerful example of what it means to truly invest in people, infrastructure, and the future of our community.
This transformative facility will no doubt have a positive impact on North Texas for years to come.
