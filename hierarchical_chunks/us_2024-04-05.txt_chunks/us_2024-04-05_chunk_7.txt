Mr. GRAVES.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to commemorate the remarkable journey of B&B Theatres as they mark their centennial milestone.
As we celebrate the 100th anniversary of B&B Theatres, we reflect upon a century of enriching experiences, cherished memories, and cultural contributions in my home state of Missouri, as well as communities across the U.S. Beyond its cinematic offerings, B&B Theatres has been a beacon of community engagement, fostering connections and bringing people together.
Through partnerships with local organizations, schools, and charities, B&B Theatres has demonstrated a profound dedication to giving back to the communities it serves.
Moreover, B&B Theatres has continuously embraced innovation, adapting to the evolving landscape of the entertainment industry while staying true to its core values.
From the introduction of state-of-the-art technology to the enhancement of amenities and services, B&B Theatres has consistently elevated the moviegoing experience, ensuring that patrons receive nothing short of excellence.
As we honor the legacy of B&B Theatres on this momentous occasion, let us extend our heartfelt congratulations to the founders, employees, and patrons who have contributed to its enduring success.
May the next century be filled with continued prosperity, innovation, and memorable experiences for all who walk through the doors of B&B Theatres.
