Mr. Vent has shown unwavering dedication with an incredible 56-year career in education, including 43 continuous years as a Superintendent, making him the longest continuously serving Superintendent in Arkansas.
He currently serves as the President of the Great Rivers ESC Board of Directors and Superintendent of the Clarendon-Holly Grove School District.
Mr. Vent is a strong testament to the vision, compassion, and resilience demonstrated by thousands of Arkansas educators.
