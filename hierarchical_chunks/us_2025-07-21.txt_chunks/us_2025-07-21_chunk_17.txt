Mr. Ontiveros' commitment to service extends well beyond his professional duties.
Mr. Ontiveros has taken to the front lines of community support following the countless fires that devastated Lake County, communicating important information and ensuring that Lake County residents have equitable access to emergency services and necessities during major disasters.
Mr. Ontiveros is an ordained minister and faith leader.
He works as a preacher in Middletown for the South Lake County community.
