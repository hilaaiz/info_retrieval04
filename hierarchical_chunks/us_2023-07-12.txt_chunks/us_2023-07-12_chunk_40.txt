Mr. FITZPATRICK.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the contributions of a scholarly article recently published by the Jewish Review of Books, titled ``When Freedom Began to Ring.''
Authored by Paul Finkelman and South-Eastern Pennsylvanian Lance J. Sussman, this article explores how the patriotism of Jews during the American Revolution contributed to political equality and a national policy of religious liberty in the newly formed nation.
Religious liberty is a critical component to our identity as Americans and this research highlights the important contributions of Jews in shaping this culture full article by Paul Finkelman and Lance J. Sussman.
