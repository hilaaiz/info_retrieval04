Mr. BILIRAKIS.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and honor the unique contributions of one of my constituents, 
Ms. Janine Kell.
Ms. Kell is an engaged and dedicated volunteer with Warrior 321, an antihuman trafficking awareness group.
Most recently, Janine was instrumental in hosting the community's second annual Light Up the Night Hernando event to bring awareness about human trafficking to the county.
Janine never sleeps and has truly dedicated her life in every way to fighting on behalf of the vulnerable.
In addition to her work to prevent the horrific crime of human trafficking, Janine regularly speaks at local schools, educating students on the Monique Burr curriculum.
