Ms. SEWELL.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor the extraordinary life and legacy of a Civil Rights icon and community leader, Bishop Calvin W. Woods, Sr., who passed away on August 16, 2025, at the age of 91.
A native of Alabama's 7th Congressional District, Bishop Woods was born on September 13, 1933, in Birmingham, Alabama, to the late Maggie Rosa Lee Wallace Woods and Abraham Lincoln Woods, Sr. Bishop Woods received his Bachelor of Arts from Miles College before continuing his education at Birmingham Baptist College.
He went on to complete multiple degrees at the Universal Bible Institute, Union Baptist Seminary, and the School.
Woods is also a graduate of the Urban Training Center for Christian Missions and obtained a certificate for police communications from Samford University.
