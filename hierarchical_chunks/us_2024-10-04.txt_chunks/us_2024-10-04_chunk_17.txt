Mr. CARTER.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to mourn the loss of Vernon ``Leon'' Davis.
Leon Davis was a firefighter for over 30 years in Blackshear, Georgia.
Mr. Davis worked his way up to Assistant Fire Chief of Blackshear while serving part time over his expansive 30-year career.
Sadly, Davis passed in the line of duty while helping his Blackshear community during Hurricane Helene.
This Hurricane has hit Georgia and the Southeast region of United States hard.
