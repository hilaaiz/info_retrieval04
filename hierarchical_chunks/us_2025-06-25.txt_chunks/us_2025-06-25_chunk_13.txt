In August 2003, 
Mr. Moore transitioned to Social Security Headquarters in Washington, D.C. serving as National Public Affairs Specialist, Human Resource Specialist, and Interactive Video Training (IVT) trainer, and Systems Analyst.
While at headquarters, 
Mr. Moore was instrumental in developing and revising scripts for IVT broadcasts aired nationwide.
He provided leadership and training to new employees on related programs.
During his tenure in this role, 
Mr. Moore traveled the United States engaging with groups such as disability services organizations and Native American organizations.
He prepared meetings for both the Commissioner and Acting Commissioner with White House staff to discuss Social Security Administration initiatives.
From August 2003 through April 2025, 
Mr. Moore served in the Chattanooga, Tennessee Field Office as a Public Affairs Specialist.
In this role, 
Mr. Moore has served as constituent liaison to my office providing outstanding service and support both to my district staff and to my constituents.
Now, Arnoldo enters a new chapter in his life and is looking forward to spending more time with his wife, Yaritza, and his children, Aniza and Alejandro, while continuing to serve in our community.
It is with great honor that I recognize 
Mr. Arnoldo Moore for his many years of public service to our Nation and our community, and I wish him well in all new endeavors as he begins his retirement.
