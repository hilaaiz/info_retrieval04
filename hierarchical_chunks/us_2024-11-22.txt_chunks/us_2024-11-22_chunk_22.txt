Mr. LaMALFA.
Mr. Speaker, I rise to honor the public service career Nick is a third generation Sutter County resident and farmer.
The Micheli family roots in Sutter County go back to when the family first started farming in Live Oak in the 1920's.
Nick's early education was in Sutter and Yuba County where he attended Sutter Union High School.
and then Yuba College.
Nick would further his education at the University of Phoenix, where he majored in Business Management and received an Associate's Degree in Business Fundamentals.
Nick would take over the family farm, Micheli Enterprises Inc., at the age of 20, after his father passed away.
Nick continues to serve as the Manager and Owner of the farming operation and as the Secretary and Treasurer of the Lomo Receiving Co. in Live Oak.
Nick would also serve as a Board Member of the Yuba-Sutter Farm Bureau and California Dried Plum Board.
In these organizations, Nick served on the Government Affairs and Management Committee.
Nick proudly serves as a local ambassador to Shriner Children's Hospital and is a 32nd Degree Free Mason who chairs the bicycle program for local elementary schools.
Nick and his wife, Sasha, have a daughter named Violet.
We thank Supervisor Nick Micheli for his years of service advocating for the agricultural industry while on the Sutter County Board of Supervisors.
We wish him all the best in his career.
