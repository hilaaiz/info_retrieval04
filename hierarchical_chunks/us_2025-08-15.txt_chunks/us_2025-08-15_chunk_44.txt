Mr. Parker attended Moravian College, where he earned a bachelor's degree in musical performance and studied wind conducting, choral conducting, and saxophone under renowned academics and musicians.
During his time there, he co-founded both the Moravian College Jazz Quartet and the Moravian College Dixieland Band.
His exceptional talent earned him the lead alto saxophone positions with the Moravian College and Muhlenberg College Big Bands, and he also served as Principal Alto Saxophone for the Moravian College Wind Ensemble.
His pursuit of excellence in music continued through his master's education at Wilkes University and additional graduate coursework at Villanova University, University of Miami, University of Arts, and West Chester University.
