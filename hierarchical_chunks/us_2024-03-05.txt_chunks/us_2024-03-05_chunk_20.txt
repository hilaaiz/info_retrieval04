Senator Holzendorf passed away on February 29, 2024 at the age of 84.
As a lifelong resident of Jacksonville, 
Senator Holzendorf dedicated her life to the betterment of her community.
She received her bachelor's degree from Edward Waters University and her master's in educational administration from the University of North Florida, the latter of which has a road named after her on campus.
As a former public school teacher and administrator at her alma mater, Edward Waters University, she was committed to quality education and began her career in public service in 1975 as an affirmative action officer under Jacksonville Mayor Hans Tanzler.
