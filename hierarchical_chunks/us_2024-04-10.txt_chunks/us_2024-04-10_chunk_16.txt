Mr. FALLON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize 
Mr. William ``Bill'' Douglass of Sherman, Texas, and present him with the Congressional Patriot Award.
Bill is a distinguished American who has faithfully served our community and country.
A native of Pennsylvania, Bill graduated from Muhlenberg College in 1958 as a Dean's List Scholar.
He later joined the United States Marine Corps and Army, serving as a First Lieutenant specializing in Combat Intelligence.
