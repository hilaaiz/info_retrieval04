Representative Delahunt always put the needs of his constituents first.
When low-income residents in his home state struggled to pay their heating bills during the winter of 2006, he got little assistance from the Bush administration.
Instead of doing nothing, he famously brokered an international fuel sale for approximately 50,000 Massachusetts residents in dire need.
His direct negotiation of this sale ensured even his most vulnerable constituents could keep the heat on that winter.
