Mr. Gable was elected chairman of the Republican Party of Kentucky in 1986, at a time when Republicans were vastly outnumbered.
A strong and unwavering leader, he began to lay the groundwork for changing the political landscape in Kentucky.
His enthusiasm, optimism, and gracious demeanor never diminished.
He took a close personal interest in candidates and party leaders, giving expert advice and counsel.
Over the years he served as a mentor for countless young people with an interest in politics.
