Mr. Speaker, Ahmari Taylor's contribution to the Dyett Eagles' journey from near closure to state champions is an inspiring narrative for us all.
It underscores the critical importance of supporting our public schools and investing in programs that empower young people to achieve their fullest potential.
I ask my colleagues to join me in congratulating Ahmari Taylor and the entire Dyett High School boys' basketball team on their momentous achievement and honoring them for the inspiration they provide to communities across our Nation.
