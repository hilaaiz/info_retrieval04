Mr. WILSON.
Mr. Speaker, today I am grateful to honor the remarkable life of 
Mrs. Beverly Jean LaHaye, founder, and former chairman of Concerned Women for America (CWA), the Nation's largest public policy women's organization.
On Sunday, April 14, 2024, she passed from this life, leaving behind a legacy that will endure through the ages, shaping the lives of countless individuals.
I am grateful for the profound impact 
Mrs. LaHaye left upon us all and her unwavering commitment to God and public ministry and offer my sympathy to her family.
