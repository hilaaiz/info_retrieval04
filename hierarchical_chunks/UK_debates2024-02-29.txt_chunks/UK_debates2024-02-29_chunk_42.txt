The emotional aftermath and devastated lives that are left after violent knife crime have been highlighted in a recent, very powerful documentary titled “Grief”, produced locally in the west midlands by the Express and Star.
It is a really poignant reminder of the call for action and awareness, and features the brave stories of two grieving families—two sets of parents, including one from my constituency.
I know that the Home Secretary takes this issue very seriously, but can my right hon.
Friend provide us with an update on when legislation will be brought forward to deliver a ban on machetes and zombie-style knives?
