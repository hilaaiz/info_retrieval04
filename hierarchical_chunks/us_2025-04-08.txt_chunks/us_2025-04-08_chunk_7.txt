Mr. LARSON.
Mr. Speaker, it is my great honor to celebrate the remarkable achievements of East Hartford's own Patrick Agyemang, who recently joined the U.S. Men's National Team.
From being named a 2017 All-Conference soccer player while at East Hartford High School, to playing soccer for my alma mater, Eastern Connecticut State University, to his new position on the USMNT, Patrick's hometown has watched with pride as he represents the best of our community.
