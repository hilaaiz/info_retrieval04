The shadow Secretary of State asks about a single construction regulator.
We accepted that recommendation in principle, but the single regulator will deliver the functions recommended by the inquiry, with two exceptions to avoid conflicts of interest: setting the rules for construction products and policing its own compliance.
We will consult on the design of the regulator in the autumn.
