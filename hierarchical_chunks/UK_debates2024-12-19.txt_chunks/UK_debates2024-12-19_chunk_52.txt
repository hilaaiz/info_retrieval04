Any increase in funding for the hospice movement is of course welcome, but let us be honest: it is giving with one hand and taking with the other.
The two excellent  hospices that serve my constituency—St Andrew’s in Grimsby and Lindsey Lodge in Scunthorpe—tell me they want certainty.
The Minister says they will be told early in the new year.
Can the Minister give a categorical assurance that in the first half of January hospices will be told how much extra they are getting from the £100 million she mentioned?
