Mr. Martinez was born in 2000 to parents Hector Martinez and Jolayne Houtz.
He grew up as a brother to Ariana Martinez in Bellevue, Washington, and throughout his life he was surrounded by friends and loved ones.
Those who loved him remember his fierce athleticism on the soccer pitch, his dry sense of humor, his intelligence, his commitment to volunteer work locally and abroad, and his loyalty to his friends and family.
He was in the Bellevue School District's Spanish immersion program and graduated from Newport High School in June 2019.
In the fall of 2019, 
Mr. Martinez matriculated at Washington State University, where he planned to study business and entrepreneurship.
