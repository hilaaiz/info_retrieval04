Mr. JOHNSON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor 
Mr. Daniel A. Moore, Sr. of Stone Mountain, Georgia, who passed away on March 4, 2024.
Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, on November 20, 1935, 
Mr. Moore found his true calling in film.
After moving to Atlanta in the 1970s, he quickly became a key figure in the City's burgeoning cultural scene.
