Ms. DAVIDS.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today in support of H.R. 1240, the Winnebago Land Transfer Act, introduced by my colleague, Mr. Feenstra from Iowa.
This critical bill would transfer rightfully-owed land back to the Winnebago Tribe.
The land in question was taken from the Tribe in an illegal acquisition, and due to complex legal stipulations within the most recent court proceedings
