Mr. LANGWORTHY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and honor the service of our brave men and women for Armed Forces Day, May 17, 2025.
Armed Forces Day is a 75-year tradition to thank all of those who are currently serving, as well as those who have served and sacrificed to defend our freedom.
It is because of their duty, honor, and courage that the United States of America remains free today.
