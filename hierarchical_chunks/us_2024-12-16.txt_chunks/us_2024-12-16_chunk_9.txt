Mr. Payne moved to the Bureau of Reclamation within the U.S. Department of the Interior in 2010 and was named Reclamation's Deputy Commissioner for Policy, Administration and Budget, a role he held until his retirement.
He oversaw agency resources, policy formulation, finance, law enforcement, dam safety, program and budget, and more.
He also served as Reclamation's Chief Financial Officer.
Throughout his public service, 
Mr. Payne received numerous awards for his dedication and leadership.
He was honored with the Secretary of the Interior's Gold Leadership Award in 2007, Bronze Leadership Award in 2006, and Silver Leadership Award in 2005.
