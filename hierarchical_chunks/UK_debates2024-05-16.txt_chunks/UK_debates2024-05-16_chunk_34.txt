We continue, of course, to work with a wide range of countries and bodies—including Gulf and African partners, the Intergovernmental Authority on Development, the African Union and the UN—to achieve a ceasefire,  and at the same time and most importantly, we are increasing much-needed humanitarian assistance and access to protect civilians.
We will continue to lead on the UN Security Council, where we hold the pen on Sudan.
On  8 March , the UNSC adopted a UK-drafted Ramadan ceasefire resolution, which called for an immediate cessation of hostilities.
