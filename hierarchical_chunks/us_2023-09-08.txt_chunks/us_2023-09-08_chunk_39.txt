Mr. OWENS.
Mr. Speaker, this year, the entire state of Utah celebrates a remarkable milestone in our community's history--the 75th anniversary.
As Utah's largest college with more than 45,000 students across 10 campuses, SLCC has played a pivotal role in shaping the lives of countless individuals and has been a cornerstone of education and progress in the Beehive State.
Over the past seven and a half decades, Salt Lake Community College has been a beacon of opportunity, offering a pathway to success for students from all walks of life.
It has embraced the values of collaboration, community, inclusivity, learning, innovation, integrity, and trust, providing a welcoming environment for students of varying backgrounds and aspirations.
As we commemorate this milestone, we must recognize the dedication and hard work of the faculty, staff, and administrators who have tirelessly supported and guided our students throughout their educational journeys.
The impact of their commitment extends far beyond the walls of the classroom, touching the hearts and minds of our community members.
The successes achieved by Salt Lake Community College alumni are a testament to the institution's commitment to excellence.
Graduates have gone on to become leaders, innovators, and contributors to our society in various fields.
Their accomplishments reflect the quality of education and opportunities provided here.
I would also like to acknowledge the role of the community in nurturing and sustaining this institution.
Salt Lake Community College has thrived because of the unwavering support of the people it serves.
Your investment in education has paid dividends in the form of a more skilled and empowered workforce, contributing to the prosperity of our region.
As we look ahead to the future, let us continue to support Salt Lake Community College's mission of empowering individuals through education.
Let us rededicate ourselves to the values of learning, growth, and community that have defined this institution for 75 years.
Together, we can ensure that SLCC remains a beacon of hope and opportunity for generations to come.
Congratulations to Salt Lake Community College on this momentous occasion and may the next 75 years be even more fruitful and inspiring.
I thank them for their dedication to our community and its future.
