The Go North East bus industrial action continues, with very few buses running for weeks and, on many routes, not at all.
That is having a huge detrimental impact on our local economy and jobs, and on the learning of students who cannot get to their local further education colleges such as Gateshead College in my constituency.
Students who struggled to make up the learning lost during covid are being hit again, since they are unable to attend at all without incurring massive additional expense.
Can we have a debate in Government time about introducing a compensation scheme from public transport providers that are singularly failing in their service delivery obligations?
