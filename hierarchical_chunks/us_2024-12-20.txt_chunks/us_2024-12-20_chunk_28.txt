Currently, he is the President and Educational Management Consultant with HRS Management Services.
In addition to serving as an adjunct faculty member at Kean University and Seton Hall University, Councilman Smith was an Associate Vice President for Administration and Finance at Kean University and an aide to New Jersey State 
Senator Robert Martin.
Prior to being elected as a Councilman in 2017, he was a member of the Piscataway Board of Adjustment, the Jefferson Township Planning Board, and served as the President of the Jefferson Township Board.
Additionally, he currently serves as a Chairman of the Lake Hopatcong Commission.
A U.S. Army veteran, Councilman Smith received an honorable discharge after serving from 1954 to 1956.
His dedication to his community and country is reflected in his civic activities and professional memberships, including leadership roles with the Association of School Business Officials International and the New Jersey Association of School Business Officials.
Councilman Smith's lifelong dedication to service and leadership has left an indelible mark on the community.
I want to express my gratitude to Councilman Smith for his decades of service and for the countless ways he has enriched the lives of others, and I wish him the best for a well-earned and restful retirement.
