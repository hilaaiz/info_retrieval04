Mr. Carter graduated from Florida State University with a bachelor's degree in criminology in 1987.
He married his wife in 1988 and has three sons.
For the past 27 years, 
Mr. Carter has worked for the Orange County Supervisor of Elections in various roles.
He's organized mock student elections at various elementary and middle schools to teach the importance of voting to younger generations.
He's also served as a poll worker for the Orange County community, making sure the voting process goes smoothly for residents.
