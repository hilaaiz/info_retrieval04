One of Shanelle's greatest strengths is her positivity and maturity, which allowed her to tackle the demanding work of serving the constituents of Florida's 24th Congressional District with skill and ease.
Shanelle has concluded her internship in my office, and she leaves a legacy of excellence and service.
Her next chapter will undoubtedly be marked by 'her continued success and impactful work.
On behalf of Florida's 24th Congressional District, I ask my colleagues to join me in honoring Shanelle Animley for her drive and dedication to the office and beyond.
