As the newly appointed Treasury spokesperson for the Liberal Democrats, this is my first opportunity to welcome the Chancellor and Ministers to their places.
Notwithstanding that, on the winter fuel payment, the Government need to think again.
I recently spoke with representatives of Citizens Advice in St Albans, who are deeply concerned that letters from the Department for Work and Pensions will  be sent out only in December to people that it believes are eligible, meaning that many people may lose out.
We have urged the Government to either reverse the cut and make it taxable or look at, for example, raising the pension credit limit.
Could the Government confirm whether they are going to look again at any of the measures that we have suggested?
