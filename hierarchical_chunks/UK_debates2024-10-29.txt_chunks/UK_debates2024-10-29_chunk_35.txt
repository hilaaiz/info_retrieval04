I thank my hon.
Friend for his question.
He is absolutely right to say that pubs make an enormous contribution to our society and economy.
The current alcohol duty system supports pubs through draught relief, which ensures that eligible products served on draught are charged less duty.
The Government are committed to delivering a fairer business rates system for high streets, including hospitality.
Any decisions on future tax policy will be announced by the Chancellor at a fiscal event, the next of which is tomorrow.
