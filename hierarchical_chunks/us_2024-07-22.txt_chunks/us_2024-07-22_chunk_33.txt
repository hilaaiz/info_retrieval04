Mr. WILSON.
Mr. Speaker, today a Service of Worship in Remembrance of and Thanksgiving for Life was held for Maria Locke Boineau Macaulay at the historic First Presbyterian Church of Columbia, South Carolina.
Services were conducted by Reverend Dr. David H. Lauten, with organist Thomas Russell, and soloist Emma Hagood.
I am grateful Maria was the wife of my fellow former State Senator Alex Macaulay and my cherished Boineau second cousin.
