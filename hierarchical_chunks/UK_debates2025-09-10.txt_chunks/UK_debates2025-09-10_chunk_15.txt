What steps her Department is taking to hold technology companies accountable for the content on their platforms.
Minister, welcome.
Thank you, Mr Speaker.
Through the Online Safety Act 2023, platforms now have a legal duty to protect users from illegal content and safeguard children from harmful content.
Ofcom has strong powers to hold firms to account, including fines of up to 10% of qualifying worldwide revenue.
Ofcom has made it clear that it will act where platforms fall short, and has already launched 12 investigations into suspected non-compliance.
I assure my hon.
Friends that we will continue to review this area carefully and will not hesitate to go further.
