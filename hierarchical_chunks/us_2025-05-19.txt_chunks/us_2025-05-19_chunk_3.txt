Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate the Asian American Heritage Festival.
A cultural event that is dedicated and celebrated for the cultural traditions and contributions of Asian Americans throughout the United States.
The Asian American Heritage Festival is committed to enriching the cultural fabric of our community, fostering understanding, appreciation, and shared experiences among people of all backgrounds.
It stands as a vital bridge to connect culture, diversity and unity through storytelling, exciting performances, and delicious food.
Beyond all of the exciting attractions that the Asian American Heritage Festival offers, there is a strong focus on community and education.
