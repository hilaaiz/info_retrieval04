Senator John L. Scott, Jr. was born on October 21, 1953, in Richland County, South Carolina, to the late John L. Scott, Sr. and Gracie W. Scott.
Senator Scott graduated from C.A. Johnson High School in Columbia, South Carolina, and attended my alma mater, South Carolina State University, where he graduated in 1975 with a bachelor's degree in accounting.
He would go on to found J.L. Scott Realty Company, Inc., in 1981 and serve as owner and President of C&S Consulting Group.
I knew 
Senator Scott well through his transformational work in the state.
From 1988 to 1990, he served as Vice Chair for Richland County Council.
He was elected to the South Carolina House of Representatives in 1990, where he served until his election to the South Carolina State Senate in 2009.
