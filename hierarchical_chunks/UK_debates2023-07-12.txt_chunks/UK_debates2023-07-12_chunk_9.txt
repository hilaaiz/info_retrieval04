Many MPs use the Help to Claim service or the benefits calculator to assist constituents.
I think she will be keen to know that the Minister responsible for pensions, my hon.
Friend  the Member for Sevenoaks (Laura Trott) , announced the innovative Invitation to Claim trial, which will be held in 10 local authorities across Great Britain this summer.
It will involve the Department for Work and Pensions sending letters to 2,600 pensioner households identified by housing benefit data and most likely to be entitled to pension credit.
That is on top of the wide-ranging communications we are already doing.
