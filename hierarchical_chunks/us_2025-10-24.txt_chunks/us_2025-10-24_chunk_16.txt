Women of the Year.
Charmaine is a dedicated Community Benefit Manager, with over fifteen years of experience in public affairs and community benefit in Kaiser Permanente's Napa-Solano Service Area.
As a Community Benefit Specialist, she manages a $1 million annual budget and oversees sponsorships, in-kind donations, and staff volunteer programs.
Her work increases employee outreach and provides meaningful community engagement opportunities.
Charmaine was nominated and accepted to join the 2025-2026 Fairfield-Suisun Chamber of Commerce Board of Directors, where she advocates for initiatives that will deliver substantial economic benefits to the Solano community.
In addition to her professional role, Charmaine is an entrepreneur and community advocate.
She is the proud owner of The Vault Event Center, as well as the coowner of a local wine bar in Suisun, where she connects with community members through hospitality and shared experiences.
Balancing her professional and entrepreneurial pursuits with family life, Charmaine is a proud wife and mother of three children.
She remains deeply committed to supporting local causes, fostering community connections, and creating environments, both in her professional and personal life, where people feel seen, celebrated, and empowered.
On behalf of the people of California's 8th Congressional District, it is my pleasure to honor and recognize Charmaine Gandy for her invaluable contributions to our community.
