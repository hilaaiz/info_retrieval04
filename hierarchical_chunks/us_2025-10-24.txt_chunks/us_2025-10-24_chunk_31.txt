Mr. JOHNSON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and congratulate Gena Jorgenson of Aberdeen, South Dakota, on being named to the USA Swimming National Team in preparation for the 2028 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles.
A proud product of the Aberdeen Swim Club, Gena is an All-American at the University of Nebraska who has earned this honor through years of dedication, discipline.
and excellence in training and competition.
Her silver medal performance in the 1,500-meter freestyle at the World University Games in Germany showcased her talent on the international stage and secured her spot on Team USA.
Her journey from local pools in Aberdeen to the national stage is a testament to her determination and resilience.
Gena's success reflects the best of South Dakota: hard work, humility, and perseverance.
She has brought pride to her hometown, university, and entire state.
