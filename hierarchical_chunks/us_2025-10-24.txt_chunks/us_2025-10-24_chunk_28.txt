Whereas, Chase Crawford, a distinguished filmmaker, producer, and resident of Fort Thomas, Kentucky, has been inducted into the International Indie Filmmaker Hall of Fame at the Indie Gathering Film Festival; and Whereas, Chase Crawford over the past decade, has worked tirelessly to amplify independent voices in cinema through projects that have reached audiences both locally and internationally; and Whereas, Chase Crawford founded Four by Three Productions, which has produced a dozen award-winning films and more than fifty comedy specials, and has served as Executive Producer on groundbreaking projects; and Whereas, Chase Crawford's film Between Mercy and Me, filmed in Northern Kentucky, premiered in 600 theaters across the United States, bringing recognition to the creative spirit of our Commonwealth; Whereas, Chase Crawford, in addition to his professional achievements, has demonstrated deep commitment to service, including his volunteer work in Eastern Kentucky, where he teaches women in recovery the skills of filmmaking, photography, and editing, an effort recognized with his commissioning as a Kentucky Colonel in 2025; Now therefore, I, Thomas Massie, United States
