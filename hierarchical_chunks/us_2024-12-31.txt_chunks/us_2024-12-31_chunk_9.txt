Senator Richard Colburn passed away suddenly on Friday, December 27, 2024, in Easton at the age of 74 years old.
I would like to express my sincere condolences to his loving wife Alma, daughter Johanna Hamilton (Bobby), granddaughter Saylor and sister Marie, as well as the entire extended Colburn family and friends.
The Eastern Shore is forever grateful for 
Senator Colburn's commitment to service and his dedication to Maryland.
