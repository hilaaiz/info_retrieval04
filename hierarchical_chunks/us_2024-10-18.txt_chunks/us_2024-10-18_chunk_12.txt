Mr. Speaker, Darcel Stevens is an individual with a diverse range of accomplishments and experiences.
Not only is he a graduate of the esteemed University of Florida and a proud U.S. Army veteran, but he/she is also a talented comedian, a dedicated political-social activist, and the recent organizer of the impactful ``THE DRAG QUEEN'S MARCH 2023,'' a statewide mobilization of over 540 drag queens and their allies from across Florida to the state's Capitol, protesting against discriminatory anti-drag queen legislation, FLSB-1438.
Recognized for outstanding contributions to the community, Darcel has received numerous prestigious certificates and community service awards, including the Orlando Come Out With Pride Award, the Chuck Hummer Award, the Orlando Social Justice Award, Watermark Orlando's Most Outstanding People Honors, the 2023 Naples Pride Award, and the 2023 Rainbow Awards for Outstanding Community Service.
In the competitive realm of drag, Darcel has proven herself.
She has emerged victorious in several regional, state, and national competitions, including the titles of Miss National at Large, Miss Continental Plus, and Miss FL Universo Latina.
She is also the reigning Miss FL Babalu International 2024.
Professionally, Darcel excels as Harmony Healthcare's marketingoutreach coordinator, with a passion for providing valuable health services to marginalized communities.
