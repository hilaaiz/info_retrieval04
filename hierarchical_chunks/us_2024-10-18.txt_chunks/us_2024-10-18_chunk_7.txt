Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and celebrate the 50th anniversary.
Uniland Development was founded in a single warehouse in Tonawanda, New York, by Carl J. Montante and Nancy R. Dobson in 1974.
Since then Uniland has grown into the largest commercial real estate developer in western New York, changing the face of Buffalo and western New York through passion and dedication.
Uniland's success stems from a steadfast focus on improving the community.
With a talented team of over 200 associates, the company has consistently delivered solutions for businesses throughout the region, helping to spur economic development and growth.
Their guiding principles of integrity, professionalism, excellence, innovation, customer focus, and teamwork are not just words; they are the foundation upon which Uniland has built its legacy.
Over the past five decades, Uniland has transformed the landscape of western New York with remarkable projects that have enhanced our communities.
From the early days at 260 Wales Avenue to the expansive CrossPoint Business Park, Uniland has continually set the standard for commercial real estate.
Their developments have created thousands of jobs and revitalized neighborhoods, ensuring that both businesses and communities thrive.
Notable milestones, such as the transformation of the Elm-Oak corridor in Buffalo and the iconic Avant mixed-use redevelopment, showcase Uniland's commitment to innovation and community improvement.
