Mr. RULLI.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate the success of Leona Ave Middle School in Shadyside, Ohio, as they were recognized as a National Blue Ribbon School for 2024.
I thank the Shadyside Local School District and their determined, compassionate, and caring staff for guiding students to success.
I am proud to be able to honor Leona Ave Middle School for their exceptional work as upstanding academic leaders in the community.
The teachers and faculty not only strive for students to achieve their academic goals but are also invested in the well-being of each student.
The educators of this district devote excessive amounts of their time and energy to the success of their learners because they understand the great impact it will have on the students' lives and the advancement of their region.
I want to acknowledge and thank the Shadyside Local School District's gifted teachers and staff for their dedication to the prosperity of Ohio's youth.
I ask my colleagues in the United States House of Representatives to join me in recognizing these honorable leaders, pillars of Leona Ave Middle School in Shadyside, Ohio, as we congratulate them on their accomplishments and their contributions to the community.
These admirable citizens illustrate the preeminent standard of the Ohio educators I am proud to represent.
May their work continue to be a blessing to our industrious Nation.
