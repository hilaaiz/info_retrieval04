First elected to the Culpeper Town Council in 2014, Councilwoman Taylor has served with purpose and compassion.
Currently, Councilwoman Taylor serves her constituents through her active participation in various committees, including as a member of the Town's Personnel and Ordinance Committee, as the Council 
Representative to the Culpeper Renaissance, and as a member of the Town's Planning Commission.
She also serves as a board member.
Previously, she served as the Chairwoman of the town's Public Safety, Public Works, Planning & Community Development Committee, as a member of the Town/ County Interaction Committee, and as Council 
Representative on the Rappahannock Rapidan Regional Commission.
A pillar of excellence in the community, Councilwoman Taylor can always be found volunteering her time at Culpeper Renaissance, Inc. downtown events or going above and beyond to connect and deliver results for Culpeper.
She brings joy and kindheartedness to every community event and has been a bright light felt by us all.
She has been a dedicated advocate for all who live in Culpeper and is well deserving of the highest honor and recognition.
