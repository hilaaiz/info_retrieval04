Ms. VAN DUYNE.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to thank Senior Pastor Chul Lee for his service with the Dallas Central Methodist Church and to congratulate him on his retirement.
Pastor Lee's exceptional leadership at the Dallas Central Methodist Church has brought about remarkable initiatives that have made a significant impact on our community.
One of the standout achievements during his tenure has been the church's dedication to providing scholarships to various segments of our community.
Over the past 15 years, these scholarships have totaled an impressive $500.000, making it possible for numerous young individuals to pursue their educational aspirations.
These segments include students attending university from the Irving Police Department, students from the firefighting community, and students from Irving Senior High School who are seeking higher education.
Pastor Sung Chul Lee's unwavering commitment to our community's welfare serves as a testament to his profound dedication to the wellbeing of our citizens and his faith.
Through his leadership and the contributions of the Dallas Central Methodist Church, they have greatly improved the lives of numerous Irving residents.
It is my honor to congratulate Senior Pastor Sung Chul Lee on his successful career and retirement.
