Mr. GARAMENDI.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor Lisa NelsonBonnington, recognized as one of California's 8th Congressional District's Women of the Year.
In addition to being a prominent business leader and community advocate, Lisa currently serves as the President and Chief Executive Officer of the Fairfield-Suisun Chamber of Commerce.
With more than thirty years of experience in marketing, public relations, and organizational management, Lisa has dedicated her career to strengthening local economies and supporting small businesses across the Bay Area.
Prior to her current role, she served as President and CEO of the Martinez Chamber of Commerce and as Vice President of the Greater Concord Chamber of Commerce, where she championed initiatives to promote economic growth and workforce development.
As an active participant in the Western Association of Chamber Executives Academy, Lisa brings both expertise and passion to her work fostering collaboration between businesses, government, and community organizations.
Since joining the Fairfield-Suisun Chamber in 2024, she has focused on expanding outreach to local entrepreneurs and strengthening partnerships that enhance economic vitality for both Fairfield and Suisun City.
Lisa's work is rooted in inclusion and longterm sustainability, ensuring that every sector of the community has a voice in shaping its shared prosperity.
Through her service, Lisa continues to embody integrity, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to empowering others.
On behalf of the people of California's 8th Congressional District, it is my pleasure to honor and recognize Lisa Nelson-Bonnington for her outstanding leadership and unwavering dedication to economic and community development.
