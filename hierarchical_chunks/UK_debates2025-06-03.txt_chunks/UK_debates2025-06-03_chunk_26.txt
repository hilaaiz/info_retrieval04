This Government’s plan to support women is clear and ambitious.
The aim is to reduce the number of women going to prison.
Our Women’s Justice Board will support that.
The independent sentencing review’s recommendations on short, deferred and suspended sentences for women, which we have accepted in principle, will reduce the number of women in prison.
We have also taken decisive action by immediately accepting Susannah Hancock’s recommendation no longer to place women in young offenders institutions.
