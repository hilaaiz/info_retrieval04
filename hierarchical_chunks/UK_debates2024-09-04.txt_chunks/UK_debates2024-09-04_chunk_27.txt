The Rosebank oilfield will provide more than £6 billion of investment in UK-based businesses.
The Jackdaw oilfield will cater for the energy needs of 1.4 million UK households.
This Labour Government are jeopardising all that investment, energy and jobs by dropping the UK Government’s opposition to the judicial review, which aims to block these vital energy projects.
Can the Secretary of State seriously tell the 90,000 people whose jobs rely on oil and gas in Scotland that the future of this crucial industry is secure under Labour, when it is his Government’s policy to oppose all new developments?
