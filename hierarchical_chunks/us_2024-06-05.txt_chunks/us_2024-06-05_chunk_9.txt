Mr. EMMER.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and honor the distinguished career and dedicated community service of Sauk Rapids Mayor Kurt Hunstiger who recently announced his retirement from public office.
Mayor Hunstiger's tenure in public service is truly commendable.
Over the past 30 years, he has served in various positions, including 18 years as a city council member and the last eight years as mayor.
His leadership has been instrumental in shaping the community, as he guided Sauk Rapids through times of growth and change.
Despite retiring from his day job in finance four years ago, Mayor Hunstiger continued to dedicate his time and energy to the betterment his retirement, Kurt expressed his gratitude for their support and collaboration over the years.
In this next chapter he plans to spend more time with his family.
Under Mayor Hunstiger's stewardship, Sauk Rapids has thrived.
He leaves behind a legacy of fiscal responsibility and a commitment to community engagement.
His efforts secured the city's fiscal stability, ensuring a strong foundation for the future.
His dedication, vision, and hard work over the years have made the Sauk Rapids community a better place to live, work, and raise a family.
We are sincerely grateful for Mayor Hunstiger's service and contributions to Sauk Rapids and Benton County and wish him a fulfilling and joyful retirement.
