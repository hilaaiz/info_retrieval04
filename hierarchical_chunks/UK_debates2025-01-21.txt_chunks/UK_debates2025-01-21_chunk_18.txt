This is an important issue for south Wales families.
My uncle Jacky was a deputy at Cwm Marine pit and my uncle Georgie was a deputy at Oakdale.
Sadly, they have now passed, but they were members of the British Coal pension scheme.
Many of their mining friends will be in their 80s and 90s, and their pensions deserve uplifting, so will the Minister please commit to making good progress here?
Time is running out for the men and women who fired our industrial past.
