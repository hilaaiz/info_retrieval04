What steps he is taking to tackle fly-tipping.
Under the previous Government, fly-tipping skyrocketed by 20%, leaving communities buried under an avalanche of rubbish.
This Government are clearing up their mess, tackling the waste cowboys, closing the loopholes that allowed waste crime to flourish and cleaning up Britain.
We will hunt down the fly-tippers with the latest technology, including drones and mobile CCTV, introduce new powers to seize and crush vehicles and increase prison sentences to up to five years for those transporting waste illegally.
