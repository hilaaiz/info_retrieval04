Mr. THOMPSON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor a remarkable unsung hero, Dr. Willie J. Reece, Jr.
Dr. Willie J. Reece, Jr. is a native of Grenada, MS and the youngest of six children.
His parents Willie Reece, Sr. and the late Sharon Seals Reece are his biggest inspirations.
Dr. Reece enjoys singing and is actively involved as a member at Greater Faith MB Church under the leadership of Pastor Alvin D. Brown, Sr.
Dr. Reece graduated from Grenada High School in 2010 and received his Bachelor of Art degree in psychology from the University of Southern Mississippi in 2014.
Later, he obtained two master's degrees from Belhaven University-- Master of Art in Teaching (2016) and Master of Education in Curriculum and Instruction (2018).
In 2019, Dr. Reece completed an educational leadership degree, and he completed the doctor of education program in educational leadership in 2023.
Dr. Reece has completed eight years of experience in education in the roles of teacher, lead teacher, MTSS coordinator, curriculum specialist, and principal.
