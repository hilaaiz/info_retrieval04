Mr. BILIRAKIS.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the outstanding contributions that Jerry Phillips has made through his volunteerism with the Area Agency on Aging of Pinellas and Pasco Counties.
Jerry Phillips began his journey with the SHINE program in 2013 and with his easy manner and tremendous amount of patience he has put Pasco & Pinellas residents at ease about their Medicare options.
SHINE, (Serving Health Insurance Needs of Elders) is a statewide, volunteerbased program offering free Medicare and Health insurance education, counseling, and assistance to people with Medicare and their families and caregivers.
Since 2013, Jerry has demonstrated his commitment and dedication to serving seniors through the SHINE Program.
Jerry has provided more than 2,000 hours volunteering since 2013.
