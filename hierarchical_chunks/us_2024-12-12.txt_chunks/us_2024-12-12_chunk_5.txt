Mr. CORREA.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate the career and professional achievements of Diana Gomez, who spent over 36 years as an electrical engineer and contributed to our community as part of the California Department of Transportation (Caltrans).
Born in Fresno and raised in the City of Parlier, 
Ms. Gomez graduated from Parlier High School and was named as the first member of the Parlier Unified School District Hall.
She earned her bachelor's degree in electrical engineering at California State University, Fresno.
