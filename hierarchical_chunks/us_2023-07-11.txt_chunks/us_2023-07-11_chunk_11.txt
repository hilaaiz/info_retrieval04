Mr. Ding began his career as a tax practitioner for the Internal Revenue Service in 2007.
He then opened his small business, Unicom Tax Services, with a focus on tax representation.
In 2010, he helped establish a free tax clinic at La Luz Center in Sonoma.
He has served on the Sonoma Valley Citizens Advisory Commission and is currently serving on the Sonoma City Council.
Mr. Ding has been a champion for the interests of California taxpayers.
Mr. Ding is a member of the National Association of Enrolled Agents, and has been an outspoken advocate for taxpayers, traveling to the United States' Capitol on multiple occasions to advocate to amend the Tax Cut and Jobs Act and most recently to increase the IRS funding and tax reform.
