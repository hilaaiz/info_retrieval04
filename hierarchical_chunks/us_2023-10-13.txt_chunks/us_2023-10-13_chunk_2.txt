Mr. VAN DREW.
Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, today, I honor the unwavering dedication and remarkable milestones of State 
Senator Chris Connors throughout his career.
His commitment to public service began with his election to the Lacey Township Committee in 1984, followed by his role as Mayor from 1986 to 1990.
Senator Connors has also served as Director of the New Jersey Commission on Capital Budgeting and Planning, overseeing the state's capital budget and expenditure plans.
Elected to the General Assembly in 1989, 
Senator Connors has been a champion for his constituents.
He was subsequently elected to the Senate in 2007, and his contributions have been recognized through various honors and awards.
During my time as a state legislator, I had collaborated with Senator Chris Connors on several practical initiatives, such as establishing the Coastal New Jersey Evacuation Task Force to enhance the preparedness of coastal communities for natural disasters.
Additionally, we fought diligently to secure South Jersey representation on the New Jersey Turnpike Authority, the governing body responsible for overseeing the Garden State Parkway.
