Mrs. McCLAIN.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and celebrate the 35th Anniversary of Blue Water Habitat for Humanity, a remarkable organization serving the people.
Clair and Sanilac Counties with integrity, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to strengthening our communities through the power of homeownership.
Founded on the belief that every individual deserves a safe, decent, and affordable place to live, Blue Water Habitat for Humanity has never given away homes--it has built opportunities.
Through its ``hand-up, not a handout'' model, it has empowered hardworking families to achieve independence by requiring sweat equity, responsible financial preparation, and a sustainable mortgage.
This principled approach has helped countless families put down roots and build brighter futures.
Blue Water Habitat is a community institution.
It is locally funded, locally operated, and deeply invested in the well-being of the people it serves.
While affiliated with Habitat for Humanity International and Habitat Michigan, every dollar donated stays right here, supporting neighbors and revitalizing neighborhoods across Port Huron, St. Clair, and Sanilac County.
Beyond new home construction, Blue Water Habitat provides critical home repair programs, ensuring seniors, veterans, and families facing hardship can maintain safe living conditions.
