Mr. MFUME.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the artistic brilliance of legendary filmmaker and beloved Baltimorean, John Waters, for his outstanding contributions to the film industry.
Affectionately dubbed the ``Pope of Trash'' by William S. Burroughs, 
Mr. Waters is a renowned author, producer, and director who was honored in 2023 with a star on the iconic Hollywood Walk.
His unconventional interpretations of the world color his comical and flamboyant films, and he has dedicated his career to what he playfully refers to as ``filth'' for the big screen.
An all-time great Baltimorean, 
Mr. Waters has left an indelible mark on the entertainment industry.
His love for storytelling was developed as a child, putting on puppet shows at children's birthday parties.
Through his teen years, he mastered the skills needed to write sidesplitting scripts, skills that he would put to the test for the rest of his lengthy career.
Waters' hometown in the greater Baltimore area would become a central character in many of his films.
