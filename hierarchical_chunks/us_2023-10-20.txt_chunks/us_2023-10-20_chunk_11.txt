Mr. MORAN.
Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, I rise today to recognize the 175th anniversary of Carthage, Texas.
Carthage, a vibrant and historic city nestled in the heart of East Texas, is celebrating its 175th anniversary on November 11, 2023.
This remarkable milestone is a testament to the resilience, spirit, and rich heritage that has shaped the community of Carthage over the years.
Since its founding in 1848, Carthage has grown from a humble settlement to a thriving city that stands as a beacon of progress and unity.
The city's commitment to fostering a strong sense of community and preserving its unique heritage is truly commendable.
Carthage stands as a shining example of what can be achieved when a community comes together with a shared vision and a commitment to excellence.
I urge all of my colleagues to join me in recognizing and celebrating the 175th anniversary of Carthage's founding.
Congratulations to Carthage on this momentous milestone.
May the next 175 years be filled with continued growth, prosperity, and community pride.
Happy anniversary, Carthage.
