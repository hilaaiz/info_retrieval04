Mr. JOHNSON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the 2024 Truman Scholars.
This year, the Foundation received 709 applications from 285 institutions.
After a review of the students records of leadership, public service, and academic achievement the independent selection panel selected 60 students from 54 U.S. colleges and universities to receive this honor.
