This prestigious accolade is a testament to 
Mr. Gray's exemplary leadership, dedication, and contributions to the aviation industry.
During his tenure as the airport manager of Denton Enterprise Airport from 2017 to 2023, 
Mr. Gray demonstrated exceptional proficiency in managing the day-to-day operations, planning, and development of one of Texas' busiest airports.
Under his guidance, Denton Enterprise Airport grew significantly, rising from 7th busiest airport in Texas as he arrived, to become the 5th busiest airport in Texas and among the top 60 nationwide, with over 212,000 operations in 2023.
