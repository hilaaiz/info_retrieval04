The strategy to halve violence against women and girls is for every single part of our country.
The Government recognise that victims in rural and remote areas face particular barriers in fleeing abuse and accessing support.
We are already driving forward a range of activities that will support rural victims.
The Victims and Prisoners Act 2024 will require local commissioners to develop joint needs assessments for  victims of domestic abuse, to identify gaps in support.
I encourage all Members to engage with that process when it comes to their local area, and I will happily work with rural MPs in this House to make sure that that is the case.
