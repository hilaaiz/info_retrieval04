I thank the Chair of the Science, Innovation and Technology Committee for her question.
She will know that our first commitment in the Labour party manifesto at the last election was to fiscal responsibility.
It is the bedrock of this Government and the bedrock of every decision we take.
As the Prime Minister set out in his plan for change, the reason people will know the difference that a Labour Government make is that they will have more money in their pockets by the end of this Parliament than when it started.
