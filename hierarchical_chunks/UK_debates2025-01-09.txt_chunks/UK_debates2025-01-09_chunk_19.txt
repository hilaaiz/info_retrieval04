I thank the Secretary of State for her answer and welcome her to her place.
Ely Junction is a major bottleneck in our rail network and the Ely area capacity enhancement scheme is designed to improve that.
If the scheme goes ahead, it could deliver over a quarter of a million extra rail passenger journeys and take 98,000 lorry journeys off the road every year.
It will also help the midlands and the north grow their economies, because it will improve freight transport to and from ports, the midlands and the north.
To add to all those benefits, the business case stacks up: every pound invested will deliver nearly £5 of benefits.
