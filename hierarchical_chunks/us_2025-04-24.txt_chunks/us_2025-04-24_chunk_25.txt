Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and honor the Jolly Boys of Williamsville, New York, a remarkable group of volunteers whose dedication to serving their community has been a shining example of generosity and selflessness for over five decades.
What began in 1971 as a small group of parents from the Village of Williamsville coming together to support their children's involvement in local sports has grown into a community-wide effort to assist those in need, making a lasting impact on countless lives.
The origin of the name Jolly Boys is a charming pan of the group's history.
