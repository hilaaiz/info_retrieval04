Mr. NICKEL.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor our 2024 Congressional Medalist recipient from North Carolina's 13th Congressional District, Hemharsith Sivakumar Gayathri.
At such a young age, 
Mr. Gayathri has demonstrated tremendous leadership, academic excellence, and commitment to his community.
Hemharsith has been recognized for his academic and community contributions with the Outstanding Academic Excellence Award from the President of the United States and the Community Oscar Award for Outstanding Student of the Year.
In a testament to his determination and skill, Hemharsith is an FAAcertified glider pilot, who embarked on his journey in gliding at the age of 14 on August 14, 2021.
He achieved a significant milestone on October 22, 2022, by completing his Glider Solo.
His dedication and perseverance led to the attainment of his Glider License at the age of 16 on July 2, 2023, marking a remarkable milestone in his aviation journey.
Hemharsith has showcased exceptional leadership by founding the South Garner High School Cadet Titan Corps and actively engaging in the Civil Air Patrol, where he achieved the esteemed rank of Cadet 1st Lieutenant and was honored as the recipient.
Hemharsith's dedication to community service is evident through his roles as a junior volunteer firefighter at Fairview Fire Department, a certified Red Cross lifeguard, and an active volunteer in various flying associations.
Hemharsith is also a leader in academics and is ranked among the top few in his class at South Garner High School.
He has committed himself to excel further in learning and is a high-achieving student in the CCP program at Wake Tech Community College.
