honor to recognize Arizona State 
Representative Joseph Chaplik from Legislative District 3, covering the northeast valley in the Phoenixmetro area.
For almost three decades, Joseph has been a successful business executive.
He is the founder of Joseph Bernard Investment Real Estate, handling more than one billion dollars of sales volume.
The Portland Business Journal recognized him as one of the Top 40 Under 40 Business Leaders, and he has won awards including Business Ethics Finalist and Top Corporate Philanthropy.
As a member of the Arizona Legislature, 
Representative Chaplik has sponsored many bills that reflect the values of our great state, including proposals to enhance our border security, election integrity, and economic freedom.
Arizona's 8th Congressional District is thankful for Representative Chaplik's endeavors on behalf of his district and our state.
We are fortunate to have someone of his caliber and achievements in the State Legislature.
