Ms. LEE.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor retired Colonel Jerry A. Brown for a career of service to our nation.
Col. Brown is a 1996 graduate from the Army ROTC program at Emporia State University, and the second generation of his family to serve in the United States Army.
He has faithfully served our Nation for over 30 years, starting as an enlisted Petty Officer in the United States Navy before commissioning as an Officer in the United States Army.
During his career, Col. Brown deployed to the Mediterranean while serving on board the USS Forrestal CV-59 in April 1988, he conducted operations to support the Persian Gulf War, and he participated in combat in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom from 2010 to 2011.
