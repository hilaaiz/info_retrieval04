Mrs. Carlow was born on April 27, 1944, in Maud, Texas, to H.J. ``Scooter'' and Hazel Smith.
From a young age, she was known for being an academically gifted student.
In 1962, 
Mrs. Carlow graduated from Maud High School as the valedictorian.
She later received a bachelor's degree from Stephen F. Austin State University and a master's degree from East Texas State University.
After graduation, 
Mrs. Carlow briefly taught home economics and worked as an elementary school teacher before becoming a bookkeeper for Dairyland Farms and Texarkana Tractor.
