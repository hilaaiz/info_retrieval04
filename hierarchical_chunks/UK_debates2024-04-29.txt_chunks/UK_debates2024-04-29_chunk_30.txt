The Secretary of State is well aware of the issues we have with Academies Enterprise Trust and Maltings Academy in Witham town.
She will know of the stories of children missing out on school time because of exclusion and bullying.
Some are even self-harming.
What assurance can she give to pupils and  their families, who have very little choice as to which schools they go to locally, that their concerns will be heard and that they will have greater educational choice over which school their children go to?
I know we have corresponded on this recently, and I know my right hon.
Friend is taking a close personal interest and has been involved directly and personally in multiple cases.
In my most recent letter—I am not sure if it will have arrived yet—I have said that we will as a Department work with her.
