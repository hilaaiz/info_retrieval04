I welcome the Chancellor’s “build, build, build” policy, but with temporary housing in London costing £4 million a day, soaring rents and a frozen local housing allowance, it is no wonder that London councils fear going bankrupt and having to rehouse people from  outside the capital, where only 5% of homes are affordable.
In the comprehensive spending review, will my right hon.
Friend look into possibly uprating this stingy Tory legacy, so that Londoners are not forced out of London?
