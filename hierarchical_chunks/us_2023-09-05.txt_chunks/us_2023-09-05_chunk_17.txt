According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, approximately 7 percent contractors, who often do the same work as employees, have almost none of the workplace protections provided to employees, including health insurance, collective bargaining, retirement security and even antidiscrimination.
This bill focuses only on discrimination, from which every worker is entitled to be free.
