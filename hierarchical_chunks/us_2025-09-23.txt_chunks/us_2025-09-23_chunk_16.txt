Ms. NORTON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize and commend Michele Manatt for her service at The George Washington University Graduate School of Political Management (GSPM), where she has served on the board since 2021, and for her contributions to our Nation.
A native Washingtonian, 
Ms. Manatt graduated from the University of California, Berkeley, where she studied political economy.
After graduation, she returned to the District of Columbia to begin her career in public policy and international affairs.
