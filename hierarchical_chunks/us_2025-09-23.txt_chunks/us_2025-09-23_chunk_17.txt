Ms. Manatt served as a professional staff member on the House Committee on Foreign Affairs, where her portfolio included oversight of the United States' relationships with Canada and Mexico and countries throughout Central and South America.
She went on to serve as Senior Policy Advisor to three consecutive Assistant Secretaries of State for Western Hemisphere Affairs at the U.S. Department of State, helping to manage policy in the region.
She continued her public service as Director of Legislative Affairs at the White House Office of National Drug Control Policy.
Since 2006, 
Ms. Manatt has worked as an independent consultant, providing strategic insight into hemispheric policy and social integration.
She contributed to a multiyear initiative with the Americas Society/Council of the Americas to document and explore successful models of Hispanic integration in midsized American cities.
