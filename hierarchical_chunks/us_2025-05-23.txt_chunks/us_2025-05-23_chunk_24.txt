In 2015, Takemasa received the Foreign Minister's Commendation from Japan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, one of only 94 overseas recipients that year.
Additional honors include the Commendation Award of the Consul General, recognition by the Orange County Asian Committee, and acknowledgment from Orlando Mayor Buddy Dyer for strengthening the Sister-City relationship with Urayasu, Japan.
His lifelong dedication continues to inspire cross-cultural understanding throughout Central Florida.
