Ms. Henderson earned a Bachelor of Business Administration in Marketing and Management from Longwood University.
Following her time at Longwood, she went on to earn her Master of Business Administration in Public Administration from Old Dominion University.
Ms. Henderson has spent her entire career under the TRADOC Command at both Ft. Monroe and Ft. Eustis.
She gained valuable experience in installation Finance as a student trainee in the Fellowship Program, held various accounting positions, and served as TRADOC G8's Supervisory Systems Accountant prior to her assignment as Director.
As Director, she provided oversight in fielding, sustainment, training, and help desk support for the Army's financial systems for over 1600 end users across the command in the execution of a $5 billion annual operating budget.
Ms. Henderson also directed the TRADOC Risk Management and Internal Control Program.
She ensured sound fiscal compliance and safeguarded government resources against fraud, waste, and misuse as manager of the Anti-deficiency Act prevention/reporting program.
