Ms. Henderson holds a Level III DoD Financial Management Certification.
She has earned many significant awards and honors throughout her 42-year career, including 2 Civilian Service Achievement Medals and The American Society of Military Comptrollers Award for Meritorious Performance in Accounting and Finance.
She also received a Letter of Appreciation from the Secretary of the Army.
Our great nation is not able to function properly without the work of people like Tamara Henderson.
Few people have dedicated their entire career to Civilian Federal Service, and it is my great pleasure to offer my own congratulations for 
Ms. Henderson on the happy occasion of her retirement after 42 years of service to the United States.
Mr. Speaker, I ask you to join me in recognizing the dedicated career of Tamara Henderson.
