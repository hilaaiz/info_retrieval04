Mr. Haskins was born in Clifton Forge, Virginia on October 8, 1936.
He graduated from the old Clifton Forge High School.
He later earned an Associate's degree from Virginia Western Community College and a Bachelor of Arts degree from Roanoke College.
His service to law enforcement began in 1958 as a trooper with Virginia State Police, where he dedicated 16 years of his career.
In 1974, he became a Special Agent with the U.S. Forest Service, before accepting a position as Chief of Police for the City of Salem in 1975.
As Chief of Police, 
Mr. Haskins excelled at his job and became a fixture in the Salem community.
His contributions to Salem's police force included establishing the Drug Abuse Resistance Education program.
