Mr. HARRIS.
Mr. Speaker, it is a privilege to recognize Deputy Charles Hugh Bean as he retires after 11 years of service with the Worcester County Sheriff's Office.
Deputy Bean's career has been one of dedication, sacrifice and honor to his community and residents.
As Deputy Bean embarks on this new chapter in his life.
I would like to take this opportunity to express my appreciation for his service.
On behalf of the residents of Maryland's First Congressional District, I wish Deputy Bean a happy, healthy and exciting retirement spent around family and friends.
