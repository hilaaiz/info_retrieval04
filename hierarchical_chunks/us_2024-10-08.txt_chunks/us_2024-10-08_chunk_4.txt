Ms. BUSH.
Mr. Speaker, I, along with the people.
Louis, rise to honor the life and legacy of Officer David Lee III, a loving and committed husband, father, brother, son, and friend.
On behalf of Missouri's First District, I offer my deepest condolences to his wife, Tonya Lee, son, Khalil Lee, daughter, Saniya Lee, mother, Dorothy Williams, father, David Lee II, brother, Dane Lee, sisters, Danielle Williams and Kimberly Lee, and countless other family, friends, and loved ones.
Born and raised in the City.
Louis and a graduate of Pattonville High School, Officer David Lee III dedicated his life to the service of others by entering the St. Louis Police Training Academy in November 2005.
After being promoted to officer in 2007, David went above and beyond receiving the St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department's Award of Excellence on three separate occasions.
His dedication to his community was recognized through a Chief's Letter of Commendation, and he was honored as Officer of the Month.
Officer David Lee III, was a hero to his fellow officers and neighbors in the 6th and 7th precincts, and truly beloved by all who knew him.
He took care of his family and the people.
Louis--never wavering in his duty as an officer in a city he loved and called home.
To his wife, Tonya, he was a special person with a heart of gold and an infectious smile.
Officer David Lee III's dedication to the St. Louis community will forever be etched into a history of extraordinary service in Missouri's First District.
May his memory be a blessing to us all.
