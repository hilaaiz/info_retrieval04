Mr. McCLINTOCK.
I rise today to recognize Melinda Franca Fleming, a pillar of the Sonora community.
Melinda was born on the Hoopa Indian Reservation in Northern California and grew up in a small town called Waterford.
In her early adult life, she worked to balance part-time jobs and family obligations with her college work, earning an associate degree from Columbia College and later graduating Summa Cum Laude from Stanislaus State.
Melinda pursued a substitute teaching job prior to earning her teaching credential from the University of the Pacific.
