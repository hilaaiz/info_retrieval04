Mr. SOTO.
Mr. Speaker, Mayra La Paz is a renowned international journalist of Dominican origin, whose professional career has left a profound mark on the world of communications.
Born with an innate passion for storytelling, she began her career in her native Dominican Republic, where she quickly established herself as one of the most respected and admired voices in the media.
Her direct and empathetic style, combined with a commitment to truth, led her to excel on national television, covering topics of social, cultural, and political significance, earning the respect of both the public and her colleagues.
Motivated by the desire to broaden her horizons, she immigrated to New York City, where she continued her rise in Hispanic media as a reporter, host, and producer, achieving a genuine connection with the Latino community.
In the United States, her voice became a bridge between immigrants and opportunities, denouncing injustices, extolling achievements, and promoting education and culture as tools for transformation.
Her talent, charisma, and journalistic ethics have allowed her to excel on networks and programs with high audiences, such as Telemundo, Telemicro Internacional, Supercanal Caribe, and Channel 35 in New York, establishing herself as an opinion leader committed to the progress of her people.
She is included among the 100 most influential Dominicans abroad.
For her successful journalistic work, she has been recognized by government agencies in her country, New York, Puerto Rico, and now in Florida, as well as by private institutions.
She holds a master's degree in corporate communication from the Isabella II University in Barcelona, a bachelor's degree in communication sciences, studies in community management in social media, and coaching studies from the John Maxuel School, among other courses related to effective communication.
She is the eldest of seven siblings and has created a stable family with engineer Juan Carlos Rosario, with whom she has four children: Carlos, Joel, Junior, and Karla, as well as a granddaughter: Aria Rose.
Today, based in Central Florida, Mayra La Paz continues her legacy as the founder and director of Portada Latina magazine, a media outlet that showcases and celebrates the talent, entrepreneurship, and economic contributions of Hispanics in the United States.
Her magazine celebrates its 10th year of consistent reporting in the Hispanic market in 2025.
