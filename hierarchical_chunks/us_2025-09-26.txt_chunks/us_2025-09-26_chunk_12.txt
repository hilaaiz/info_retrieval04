Dilia's work and warm personality have earned her affection and recognition within the Central Florida community.
Her creative approach to photography has been acknowledged through several awards, including the 2013 APEIH Certificate of Appreciation, 2015 Revista Mujer Award, 2016 Latina Role Models Certificate of Recognition, 2016 Kissimmee Family Mission Inc.
Certificate of Recognition, 2016 HAPBWA Award, 2018 Portada Latina Magazine Award, 2019 Premios Boriken Award--Excellence in Artistic Creativity Award at the Puerto Rico Parade in Osceola, 2020 Access Community Center Recognition for Meals on Wheels, 2023 Leadership Awards at the Cuban Sandwich Festival, 2023 Premios Boriken Event Recognition, and El Premio Coqui de Oro Event Recognition.
As a professional photographer, Dilia has contributed to numerous publications, including Caribbean American Passport Magazine, Portada Latina, Yes Business Magazine, the Osceola Star, El Nuevo Amanecer, Enlace Magazine, Karas Magazine, Politica--Politico Magazine, Revista Mujer, El Periodico El Sol de la Florida, and the HAPBWA Foundation.
Dilia Castillo's contributions and impact on her community continue to be celebrated.
