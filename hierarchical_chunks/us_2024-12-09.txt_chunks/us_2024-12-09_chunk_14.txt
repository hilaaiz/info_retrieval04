Mr. THOMPSON.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor Jim Provenza for his exceptional career in public service and his unwavering commitment to our community.
After 16 years of service on the Yolo County Board of Supervisors and over 40 years working in state and local government, 
Mr. Provenza will soon retire, leaving a legacy of compassion, justice, and dedication to our community.
Born in Buffalo, New York, in 1955, and raised in San Diego, Mr. Provenza pursued a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from the University of California, Santa Barbara, followed by a Juris Doctor from the University of California, Davis Law School.
