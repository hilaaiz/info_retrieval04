Ms. WASSERMAN SCHULTZ.
Mr. Speaker, it is with great pleasure that I, along with Rep. Moskowitz, rise to recognize the tremendous achievements.
Diana Nyad was born in New York City on August 22, 1949, and raised in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida.
She is a legendary endurance swimmer, author, inspirational speaker and sports journalist.
She attended Pine Crest School in Ft. Lauderdale and later graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Lake Forest College.
