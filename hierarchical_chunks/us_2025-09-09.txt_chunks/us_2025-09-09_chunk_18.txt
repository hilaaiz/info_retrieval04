Mr. VAN DREW.
Mr. Speaker, it is truly an honor to recognize Councilwoman Patricia Andaloro for her many years of devoted service to the people.
She was recently celebrated at the Buena Boro Republican League dinner, a well-earned recognition of her dedication, integrity, and steadfast commitment to her community.
Pat first served as the Borough Clerk, where she became the backbone of local government.
In that role, she guided residents and colleagues alike with professionalism and care, ensuring that the daily functions knowledge of municipal operations earned her the respect and trust of everyone who worked alongside her.
Today, as Councilwoman, Pat continues to bring that same spirit of service and leadership.
She has shown an unwavering commitment to good governance, always keeping the needs of her neighbors at the forefront.
The recognition she received at the Buena Boro Republican League dinner was not just a tribute to her past accomplishments, but a reminder of the lasting impact she continues to make.
I congratulate Pat on a very well-deserved honor and I thank her for the great work she is doing in South Jersey.
God bless Pat and God bless our United States.
The greatest country on the face of the Earth.
