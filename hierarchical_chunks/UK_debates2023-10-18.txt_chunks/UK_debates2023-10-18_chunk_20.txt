Early diagnosis of cancer is key and the NHS “Help Us, Help You” campaign is seeking to address the barriers deterring patients from accessing diagnosis and treatment.
Thanks to treatments and faster detection, survival rates for breast cancer are now increasing.
Last year, more than 1 million scans were carried out, preventing an estimated 1,300 deaths from breast cancer.
This Breast Cancer Awareness Month, I encourage anyone who is invited to take up the offer of breast cancer screening.
