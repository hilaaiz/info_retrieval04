On  1 January  this year, the UK became an associate member of the Horizon Europe programme and Copernicus.
Given  our delayed start, could the Secretary of State say what steps she is taking to encourage participation by UK universities and businesses?
My right hon.
Friend is absolutely right.
I welcome the news shared just last week by Commissioner Ivanova, who said the early signs of uptake are absolutely excellent.
Indeed, some programmes are projecting an increase of over 50%.
We are not being complacent.
We have launched a comprehensive international marketing campaign, introduced a pump-priming scheme with the British Academy and already started on roadshows.
