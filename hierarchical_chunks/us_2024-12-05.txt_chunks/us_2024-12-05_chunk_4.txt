Mr. CARL.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor and congratulate Louis English for being named Career Firefighter of the Year for 2024.
Louis began his career in 1985 as a Firefighter 1 and received Firefighter 2 certification in 1999.
In 2013, he was appointed as Captain.
He retired from active fire service in March of 2016 but chose to remain as a volunteer.
In June of 2016, he was reinstated as an Active Reserve.
With 28 years of full-time service and 8 years of service in the Active Reserve, Captain Louis English has dedicated his life to helping others.
I am so proud to have such a selfless individual residing in our district.
I congratulate Louis English on this outstanding award.
