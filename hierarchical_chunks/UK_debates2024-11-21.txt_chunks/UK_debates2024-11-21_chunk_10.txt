On Monday, in her statement on bus funding, the Secretary of State said that a formula was being used to allocate funding.
She said that the formula will allocate funding “based on local need, population, the distance that buses travel, and levels of deprivation…This formula and the funding allocated is a fair arrangement, ensuring that every area of the country gets the service levels it needs”.—[Official Report,  18 November 2024 ; Vol. 757, c. 43-45.]
The formula, including the weighting given to the various factors by the right hon.
Lady, has not been published.
