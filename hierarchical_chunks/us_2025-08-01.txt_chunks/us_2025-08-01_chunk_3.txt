Mr. YAKYM.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize a remarkable milestone for a family-run business that has been a cornerstone of Indiana agriculture for a century, Creighton Brothers LLC.
This year, Creighton Brothers is celebrating 100 years in business, a legacy that began in 1925 when Hobart and Russell Creighton started a modest egg operation with just 2,500 hens.
Over the last century, this operation has grown into one of the most respected and vertically integrated egg producers in the country, with more than 3 million laying hens, feed production on 10,000 acres of farmland, and distribution to customers nationwide.
