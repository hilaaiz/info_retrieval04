Mrs. Emmanuel has been recognized in numerous ways over her career, including the 2011 Healthy Schools Program National Recognition Bronze Award and 2004 Finalist as a School Administrator of the Year for the Florida African American Education Alliance.
She is the 2008-2009 District Finalist for Principal of the Year; 2008 Urban League of Greater Miami Principal of the Year.
She is the recipient of the 2004 Distinguished Leadership Award, and a 2005 Letter of Commendation by Governor Jeb Bush as a School Recognized for Outstanding Progress--Most improvement in the State.
Under the leadership of 
Mrs. Emmanuel, Phyllis Ruth Miller Elementary is the recipient of the 2023 Merit School.
This distinction is the second highest honor Magnet Schools of America bestows and is awarded to those that demonstrate an exemplary commitment to academic standards, curriculum innovation, desegregation/ diversity efforts, specialized teaching staffs and parent and community involvement.
