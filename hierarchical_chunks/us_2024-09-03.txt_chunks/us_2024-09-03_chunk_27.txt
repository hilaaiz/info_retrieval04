Mr. Anderson was a strong advocate for the people.
He was elected County Judge Executive and served three terms in that important leadership position.
He supported numerous worthy projects, including the creation of Hollerwood ATV Park, construction projects for the Emergency Dispatch and Operation Center, the Senior Citizens Center, the Animal Shelter, Recycling Center, and jail expansion projects.
He worked on improving the infrastructure of Powell County by paving over 5 miles of gravel roads and being a part of over twenty bridge projects and flood mitigation projects.
In 2019, 
Mr. Anderson was recognized by the Kentucky Association of Counties as the County Judge Executive of the Year.
