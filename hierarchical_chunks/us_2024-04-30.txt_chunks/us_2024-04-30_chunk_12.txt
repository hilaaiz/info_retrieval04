Mr. Toombs served as a flight engineer gunner on both B17s and B24s with the 493rd bomb group of the 8th Army Air Force, also known as the ``Mighty 8th.''
He flew his first mission on June 6, 1944--D-Day--and went on to carry out over twenty successful missions.
For his heroism, Toombs was awarded the Distinguished Flying Cross, as well as several Air Medals and foreign decorations.
In 2021, he was inducted into the Arkansas Military Veterans Hall.
