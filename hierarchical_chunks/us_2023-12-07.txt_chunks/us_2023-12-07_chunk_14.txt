Mrs. O'Bannon has been a dedicated public servant on behalf of her constituents for the last 27 years, working with colleagues across the aisle to find solutions for her community.
She has been a steady leader--stepping in as Chairwoman for the fifth time in February 2017 after the passing of Brookland 
Representative Richard Glover.
Her unwavering dedication to working on behalf of Tuckahoe and all of Henrico County has left a lasting impression for years to come.
