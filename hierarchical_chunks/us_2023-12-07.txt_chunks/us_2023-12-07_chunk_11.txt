Ms. Duvall was a life-long Democrat and vigorously defended equal rights.
She volunteered on Election Day to ensure that residents could cast their vote.
After attending many memorial services to honor friends who had passed, 
Ms. Duvall decided, in January 2020, to celebrate her 85th birthday with beautiful music performed by her children and grandchildren and amusing, heartfelt anecdotes from her friends and loved ones.
Ms. Duvall leaves behind her daughter, Teresa, and granddaughter Zoe, as well as her son, Roger, his wife Kimberlea, and their children, Maximilian and Celia.
