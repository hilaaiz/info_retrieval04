Mrs. O'Bannon was first elected in 1995 to represent the Tuckahoe Magisterial District.
With her election, she became the first woman to be elected to the Henrico Board of Supervisors, where she became a strong voice for the communities she serves.
A graduate of Virginia Commonwealth University, 
Mrs. O'Bannon has proven to be a source of knowledge for the Henrico community.
Her expertise is informed by her service on the Richmond Regional Transportation Planning Organization, the Greater Richmond Partnership, and as the former Chair of the Capital Region Airport Commission.
Throughout her tenure, she has focused on bettering Henrico County's infrastructure and improving Henrico schools.
She has also been an advocate for revamping the sidewalk policy to increase public safety and accessibility to sidewalks for all in Henrico.
