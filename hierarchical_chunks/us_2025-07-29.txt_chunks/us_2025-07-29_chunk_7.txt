The records for Greene County confirm that members of the Brown family legally held property in the area now known as Browntown.

The legal documentation, when paired with oral histories passed down through generations, affirms that Browntown was not a place imagined but a living, land-based community that sustained itself through shared heritage, mutual protection, and pride in place.
