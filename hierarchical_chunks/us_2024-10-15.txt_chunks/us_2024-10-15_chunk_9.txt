Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate the inauguration.
Adiam Tsegai, SUNY Erie's 13th President.
Dr. Adiam Tsegai is an alumnus of SUNY Erie Community College where she earned her Associates Degree in Liberal Arts and Social Science.
Continuing her education at the University of Buffalo, Dr. Tsegai earned a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and African American Studies.
She also received a Master's degree in Education and received her Ph. D. in Education, Leadership Policy, and Social Foundations.
Dr. Tsegai joined SUNY Erie as the Dean of Engineering and Technologies in 2018 and then served as Associate Vice President for Academic Affairs for Health Sciences, Engineering, and Technologies.
In 2021, she was appointed as Provost and Vice President of Academic Affairs for the College.
Dr. Tsegai served as the College's Officer-inCharge, and on December 5, 2023, was appointed the President of SUNY Erie.
Today, we thank Dr. Adiam Tsegai for her commitment to higher education and serving the community.
Please join me in congratulating Dr. Adiam Tsegai on this momentous occasion and for her continued leadership and passion for education.
