Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor 
Mrs. Kimberly Collins-Jones on the occasion of her 62nd birthday.
Born on October 11, 1964, in Buffalo, New York, 
Mrs. Collins-Jones is a cherished wife to Ulysses Jones and a devoted mother of two.
She enriches the lives of her loved ones, ensuring that every gathering is filled with laughter, love, and delectable food.
In addition to her culinary talents, 
Mrs. Collins-Jones is a dedicated member of True Bethel Baptist Church, where she has devoted two decades of service.
She also plays an important role in the Paramount Order of Eastern Stars, exemplifying her commitment to community service.
As the proud owner of Phat Katz in the heart of the University District, 
Mrs. Collins-Jones has transformed her dream into a vibrant reality.
Since opening in May 2016, her restaurant has become a beloved institution in Buffalo, renowned for its soul food that nourishes both body and spirit.
It is a place where families gather, friendships blossom, and memories are created over delicious meals.
