Mr. Pease earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Agricultural Education and a Master of Arts degree in Career and Technical Education.
He began his teaching career at Fleming County High School in 2003 and is a national board-certified teacher.
He is a welding instructor at Fleming County High School and Director of Education at the Kentucky Welding Institute.
In 2023 and again in 2024, 
Mr. Pease coached welding teams from Fleming County High School who were named the Kentucky FFA State Fair Welding Champions.
Mr. Pease was appointed as a Curriculum Specialist by the Accrediting Council for Continuing Education and Training.
He is an AWS Certified Welding Inspector and is certified with OSHA, an NCCER Master Trainer, and an NCCCO Practical Examiner.
