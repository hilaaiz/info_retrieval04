Mr. BILIRAKIS.
Mr. Speaker, it is a privilege to rise today to recognize 
Mr. Theodore Gomez for his service at Withlacoochee River Electric Cooperative (WREC) and his remarkable role in the Light Up Navajo Project.
In May, a courageous team of 16 WREC employees traveled over 1,800 miles to the remote, rugged terrains of Arizona and New Mexico, where they spent two weeks installing vital electrical infrastructure for Navajo families who have lived without power for generations.
