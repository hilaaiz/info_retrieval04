Mr. SOTO.
Mr. Speaker, Joshua Collins is a passionate entrepreneur, community advocate, and devoted father who has been making a lasting impact through creativity and activism since 2010.
As the founder and owner of Collins Creative Custom Costuming, Joshua has transformed his artistic vision into a thriving business that serves theme parks and cruise lines, as well as other performers, drag artists, theatrical productions, and individuals seeking expressive designs.
His attention to detail, craftsmanship, and commitment to authenticity have made him a sought-after designer in Florida and beyond.
Joshua's contributions extend far beyond the stage.
He is a longtime leader in LGBTQ+ advocacy, with commitments to creating safer, more inclusive spaces for all.
For many years, he has played a key role in organizing Pride events throughout the community, helping to celebrate identity and foster connection.
His work has empowered others to live boldly and speak out against injustice.
He is known as a founding organizer of the Drag March to Tallahassee, a powerful movement in 2023 that brought together drag performers, allies, and advocates to protest discriminatory legislation and demand equal rights for the LGBTQ+ community.
The march has become a symbol of resistance, pride, and solidarity under Joshua's visionary leadership.
In addition to his work in the arts and activism, Joshua serves as the nonprofit director of Adopt with Pride, an organization focused on supporting LGBTQ+ individuals and families throughout the adoption process by offering educational and community-building initiatives.
Joshua is the proud father of a beautiful daughter, who inspires his work every day.
His passion for justice, creativity, and family fuels everything he does.
Through costume design, advocacy, and leadership, Joshua continues to shape a more vibrant, inclusive, and compassionate world.
