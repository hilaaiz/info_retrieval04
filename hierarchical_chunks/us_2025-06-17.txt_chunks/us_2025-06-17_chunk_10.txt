Mr. SOTO.
Mr. Speaker, Marc Espeso is a dedicated public servant, skilled communicator, and advocate for the LGBTQ+ community.
Since his appointment in August 2019, he has served as special assistant to Orange County Mayor Jerry L. Demings.
In addition, he holds the role of digital communications manager for the county.
This dual position provides him with a unique perspective that combines executive insight with hands-on communication strategy.
