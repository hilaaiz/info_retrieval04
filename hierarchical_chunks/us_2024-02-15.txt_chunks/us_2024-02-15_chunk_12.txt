Mr. Momaday will be forever remembered across Native America and New Mexico.
His writing sparked our imagination and took our hearts to places our bodies have never touched.
He had the ability to paint the picture of a story through the finesse of power in his words.
He created space necessary for creative culture to follow.
For all this and more, we recognize N. Scott Momaday for his indelible influence on the state of New Mexico and to the United States.
