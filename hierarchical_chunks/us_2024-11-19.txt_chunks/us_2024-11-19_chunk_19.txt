Mr. PFLUGER.
Mr. Speaker, I rise to recognize the passing of former Mayor Bert Massey of Brownwood, Texas on November 13, 2024.
Mr. Massey was born in Brownwood on October 3, 1943, and he was a pillar of leadership in his community throughout his life.
Mayor Massey graduated from Brownwood High School and went on to earn a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Texas in August 1967, followed by a Doctor of Jurisprudence from the University of Texas School of Law in January 1969.
He served on the District Court for the Northern District of Texas and the United States Fifth Circuit Court.
