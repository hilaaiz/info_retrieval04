This study is a pragmatic approach that balances our responsibility to reduce emissions and support American workers and mariners.
Thanks to momentum generated by the passage of the Inflation Reduction Act, offshore wind has the potential to catalyze major investments in American manufacturing and shipbuilding.
By studying opportunities to maximize the role of American mariners and shipbuilders in meeting these goals, we ensure that we do not allow the perfect to become the enemy of the good.
