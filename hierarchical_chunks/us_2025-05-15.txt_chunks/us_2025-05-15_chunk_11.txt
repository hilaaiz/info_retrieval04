Mr. Zavitz was born in Minneapolis and was a proud graduate of Orono High School.
He later attended the University.
Thomas, where he earned a degree in history and teaching.
Initially, 
Mr. Zavitz embarked on a career in real estate and business development but ultimately found his calling in education.
In 2012, 
Mr. Zavitz began his tenure at DeLaSalle High School as a social studies teacher, where his vibrant spirit, unwavering dedication, and passion for teaching radiated.
Known for his energetic and engaging classes, 
Mr. Zavitz had the unique ability to transform lessons into powerful stories that captivated his students.
His approach to teaching was rooted in authenticity and compassion, making every student feel welcome, valued, and heard.
Colleagues and students alike remember him not just for his knowledge and dedication to teaching but also for his commitment to living a life of purpose.
Jeremy is survived by his loving wife, Maud Zavitz, and their 7-yearold son, Edmund John Zavitz.
He leaves behind a legacy not only through his family but through the generations of students who carry forward his lessons of kindness, authenticity, and perseverance.
