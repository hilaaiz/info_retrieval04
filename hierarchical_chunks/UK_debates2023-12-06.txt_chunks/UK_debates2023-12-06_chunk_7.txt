What recent discussions he has had with the Welsh Government on the potential impact of the 20 mph speed limit on rural communities.
All of us support speed limits in places where there is a risk to life.
I have supported speed limits outside schools, hospitals and other places in my constituency, but the Welsh Labour Government’s policy of bringing in a 20 mph speed limit on all 30 mph roads—a blanket speed limit—is damaging for the economy.
By their own figures, they have suggested that it could create a £4.5 billion hit to the Welsh economy.
They need to think again.
