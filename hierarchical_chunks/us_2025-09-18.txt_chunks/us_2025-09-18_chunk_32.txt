Mr. Williams' long and distinguished career in banking began at a small community bank in Houghton, before spending 25 years working for larger financial institutions in Ohio and lower Michigan.
Throughout those years, he remained committed to a personal goal: returning to the Upper Peninsula to manage and grow a community bank.
For the past 25 years, 
Mr. Williams has led Upper Peninsula State Bank with steadfast commitment to both customer service and community engagement.
His passion for serving his community extends beyond his day-to-day work, as reflected in his advocacy for the industry at both the state and federal levels.
In addition to his role as President and CEO, he serves as a Michigan Federal Delegate for the Independent Community Bankers of America and is a long-time member of FIS's Strategic Planning Advisory Council.
