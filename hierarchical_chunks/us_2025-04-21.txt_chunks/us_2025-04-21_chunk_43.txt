Mr. Farabaugh graduated with a B.S. in psychology from the University.
Ten years after his graduation, he had the opportunity to start as the finance manager at his father-in-law's Honda dealership.
He moved to different dealerships within the group and rose from sales manager to general manager.
In 1998, 
Mr. Farabaugh and his brother-inlaw purchased the dealership and now share ownership and responsibilities at D-Patrick.
D-Patrick has invested hundreds of thousands of dollars annually in an array of local Indiana organizations, such as Ark Crisis Children's Center, Boone County Cancer Society, Boys & Girls Club of Evansville, Evansville Philharmonic Orchestra, Mesker Park Zoo & Botanic Garden, Ronald McDonald House Charities of Ohio Valley, Special Olympics Indiana, Tri-State Food Bank and Walk to End Alzheimer's Southwest Indiana.
