His hands-on approach to fitness training and safety education has equipped firefighters with the tools and confidence needed to perform their duties effectively under the most challenging conditions.
Lieutenant Vergara was a founding member of the Florida Firefighter Fitness Collaborative, established in 2011.
Their mission was to offer ``Wellness Mutual Aid'' to other fire departments across the state that pursued the implementation and/or maintenance of their wellness/fitness programs, such as the IAFF Wellness Fitness Initiative.
Lieutenant Vergara currently serves on the Florida Firefighters Safety and Health Collaborative, which focuses on areas of firefighter cancer prevention, health and fitness, mental wellness, and firefighter safety.
Through the FFSHC, Bert chairs the health and safety section for the state.
Bert also serves as a member of the First Responder Center of Excellence, Health, Wellness, and Fitness Advisory Committee.
He also serves as an advisor on the IAFF Wellness Fitness Initiative Taskforce.
Lieutenant Vergara continuously seeks opportunities for professional development to stay at the forefront of emerging health and safety practices.
He remains committed to mentoring firefighters, cultivating teamwork, and creating sustainable strategies to improve overall firefighter performance.
Lieutenant Vergara was recently the recipient of the University of Miami Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center, Firefighter Cancer Initiative, Commitment Award for his contributions to improve the health and wellness of Florida's firefighters.
