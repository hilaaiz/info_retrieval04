Mr. Sorensen has also demonstrated his generosity and dedication to the arts extensively in his time.
He has organized a fundraiser for the Mariposa Arts Council when they lost their headquarters in a fire, put on an annual Pink Show to raise money for breast cancer research, supported the Timberline Gallery in Oakhurst in keeping their gallery open, among many other instances of philanthropy.
Chris was a loving husband to Mitzi Christensen and loving father to their two children, Vicki and John.
He was also a grandfather and great-grandfather to 7 great-grandchildren.
