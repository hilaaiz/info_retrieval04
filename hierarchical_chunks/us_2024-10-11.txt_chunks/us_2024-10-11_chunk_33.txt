Ms. VAN DUYNE.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the extraordinary contributions.
Originally from Rio Piedras, Puerto Rico, he has called Grapevine his home since 1995.
Jorge's passion for education and unwavering commitment to his community has made a lasting impact on the Grapevine-Colleyville Independent School District.
For 15 years, Jorge served as an elected member of the Grapevine-Colleyville ISD Board of Trustees, where his leadership and vision played a pivotal role in elevating the district's academic performance.
Under his stewardship, Grapevine-Colleyville ISD achieved the distinguished rank of sixth in Texas for its advanced studies program, a reflection of his dedication to fostering academic excellence and creating opportunities for all students.
