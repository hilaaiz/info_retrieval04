Mr. PERRY.
Mr. Speaker, I'm honored to recognize Corporal (Army, Retired)
Richard Deppen on the auspicious occasion of his 100th birthday on October 21, 2024.
Richard Deppen born on October 21, 1924, was a part of the ``Greatest Generation'', serving honorably in the Second World War.
Corporal Deppen served from April 1943 through the end of the war in 1945-- serving with the 182nd Ordnance Depot Company attached to the Third Army under General Patton in the European campaign.
His sacrifice and the others, enables our Freedom as a Nation today--and we owe them all a debt that cannot be repaid.
After his honorable military career, Corporal Deppen graduated from Pennsylvania State in 1950 with a Bachelor's degree in Agronomy.
