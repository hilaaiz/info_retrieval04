Mr. VALADAO.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate 
Mrs. Amy Bush for her dedicated service to the Hanford community and to congratulate her on her well-earned retirement as Head Coach of the Sierra Pacific High School Girls Basketball Team.
Mrs. Bush, a teacher at Kings River Hardwick Elementary School in Hanford, became the Head Coach of the Sierra Pacific High School Girls Basketball program in 2015.
Under her leadership, the Golden Bears have won an impressive 213 games and maintained a winning percentage of almost 80 percent.
Over the past nine seasons, the Golden Bears have also earned two undefeated TriCounty Conference Championships and six East Sequoia League Championships.
In 2017, the team won their first California Interscholastic Federal (CIF) Section Championship and advanced to the CIF Semi-Finals.
Just one year later, 
Mrs. Bush led the team to victory at the CIF State Southern Regional and Division V State Championships-- the first in Sierra Pacific High School's history.
