Mr. SOTO.
Mr. Speaker, Sonya Stalnaker is a Union commercial painter by trade.
She started in 2001 as an apprentice and completed the Painter's apprenticeship of the IUPAT in 2005.
She has spent her last 23 years dedicated to her Union, her family, and doing what she loves: painting and helping others.
She has served in the capacity of shop steward on the job and parttime instructor at the Painter's apprenticeship.
Working hard and coming up in the ranks, she served as Financial Secretary in Local 1010 for the previous 5 years.
She was the Commercial Paint Coordinator for the Florida Finishing Trades (Painter's apprenticeship) from 2021 to 2023.
