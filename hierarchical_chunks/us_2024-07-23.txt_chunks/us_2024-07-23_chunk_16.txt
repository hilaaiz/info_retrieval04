Ms. Williams's first teaching job was at Montague Elementary School, where she taught English and Social Studies to 4th-grade students.
She later moved to Henry Louis Bourgeois High School (HLB), where she taught Business and English.
At HLB, 
Ms. Williams sponsored the Key Club of America, educating young students on the importance of school and social organizations.
After 22 years at HLB, 
Ms. Veraniece moved to John McDonogh High in New Orleans, teaching Business and Marketing Education with an emphasis on Entrepreneurship Studies.
