Mr. BERGMAN.
Mr. Speaker, it is my privilege to honor 
Mr. Larry Straus for his 74 years of dedicated service to the United States Postal Service.
A Drummond Island native, 
Mr. Straus has been a mail carrier of the USPS since 1950 and has served many Michigan communities over the past seven decades, including Mt. Pleasant and Kingsley.
Larry has served the Drummond Island community since 2000 and recently just celebrated his 95 birthday.
