This is because it has been shamed by its antisemitic decision not to light up the stadium when 1,400 Jews were murdered in the pogrom of  7 October .
Should the national lottery even fund organisations that seem to regret every death and injustice apart from the death of Jews?

May we have a debate on improving road safety?
I recently met a community speedwatch group in Bath, a bunch of highly dedicated and motivated people who stand for hours in all weathers, facing abuse.
They have caught 80,000 speeding vehicles, yet they see very little result for their hard work.
Speeding kills: a third of road fatalities are down to speeding.
Would Road Safety Week not be a perfect opportunity for such a debate to show that the Government really take the issue of road safety seriously?
