Ms. Ahmann's dedication to agriculture extends to supporting the next generation of farmers and ranchers.
She and her family have been generous supporters of youth in agriculture, donating time and raising funds for scholarships benefiting local 4-H and Future Farmers of America students.
She has been a steadfast supporter of the Napa County Farm Bureau, actively promoting agricultural policy, education, and community engagement.
In 2010, Judy and John placed a conservation easement on 1,275 acres of Running Deer Ranch through the California Rangeland Trust, permanently protecting the ranch as a working landscape while preserving its vital wildlife habitat.
They also granted a trail easement to open public access to 9,100 acres of Bureau of Land Management land that had been landlocked for more than 150 years.
