Ms. STEFANIK.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Saratoga County Sheriff Michael Zurlo, a lifelong resident of Saratoga County who has dedicated his career to keeping our communities safe, strong, and connected.
Elected Sheriff on November 5, 2013, Sheriff Zurlo brought decades of hands-on experience to the office, having served as a deputy, sergeant, lieutenant, and senior criminal investigator within the Saratoga County Sheriffs Office.
A graduate of Mechanicville High School and former Town of Stillwater Judge, Sheriff Zurlo embodies a deep understanding Over more than thirty years, he has worked tirelessly to protect the people of Saratoga County, leading with fairness, integrity, and respect for the men and women who serve beside him.
His leadership has advanced several initiatives that have had a lasting impact on the county's safety and quality of life.
He placed school resource officers in districts across the county to ensure students and staff have trusted and visible protection.
He expanded street patrols to strengthen community policing and enhance public safety.
He modernized the county's emergency communications system to improve coordination among first responders.
He also built partnerships with local, state, and federal law enforcement agencies to better combat crime and support shared missions.
Sheriff Zurlo has also demonstrated a strong commitment to innovation and compassion.
He implemented stricter penalties for those charged with driving while intoxicated, launched the ``Blue Envelope'' program to assist drivers on the autism spectrum during traffic stops, and oversaw the opening of the county's $20 million Paul E. Lent Public Safety Facility.
Under his leadership, the Sheriffs Office introduced programs in the county jail designed to support veteran inmates and individuals struggling with addiction, offering accountability, rehabilitation, and a path forward.
On behalf of New York's 21st Congressional District, I am honored to recognize Sheriff Michael Zurlo for his dedicated service, steady leadership, and lifelong commitment to the people.
