Whether she has made an assessment of the potential merits of jobcentres launching outreach initiatives in local communities.
Jobcentres work with a range of external providers to offer a wealth of outreach support.
In Berkshire, this includes work coach support for customers with complex needs delivered by Reading college, outreach delivered by the Slough homelessness team and at Windsor Homeless Project locations, and employment support delivered by the probation hub in Reading.
Outreach work is something that we not only strongly support, but actively encourage.
