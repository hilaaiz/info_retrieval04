Ms. BUDZINSKI.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Workers Memorial Day 2024 and the workers who have both lost their lives or risk their lives in unsafe working conditions on a daily basis.
Too many people across the country, even today in 2024, are still forced to choose between a paycheck and having a safe and healthy workplace.
This is unacceptable, and on Workers Memorial Day we remember all the workers who have been injured or have lost their lives on the job.
