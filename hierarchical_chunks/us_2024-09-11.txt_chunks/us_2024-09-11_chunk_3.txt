Mr. MOSKOWITZ.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize 
Ms. Carol B. Dover, the 2024 inductee into the Florida Tourism Hall.
Since 2001, this honor has been awarded annually to figures whose vision has positively and significantly impacted the development of Florida's tourism industry.
Since 1995, 
Ms. Dover has served as President and Chief Executive Officer of the more than 10,000-member Florida Restaurant and Lodging Association (FRLA).
In 1989, 
Ms. Dover was appointed Deputy Chief of Staff within the Executive Office of the Governor.
She was also the first woman to serve as Chief of the Bureau of Fire Prevention in the Florida State Fire Marshall's Office.
