Mrs. McCLAIN.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor the extraordinary life of Russell Matar, who I named Veteran of the Month for October from Michigan's 9th Congressional District.
Born on October 18, 1924, in Port Huron, Michigan, Russell was the second of five children.
His journey began with resilience and dedication, culminating in his graduation from Port Huron High School in 1942, before he enlisted in the United States Navy in 1943.
