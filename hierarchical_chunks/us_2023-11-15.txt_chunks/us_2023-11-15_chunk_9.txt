Mr. TURNER.
Mr. Speaker, November 10, marked the 5-year anniversary of the passing of a beloved friend and colleague, former House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence Staff Director, Damon Nelson.
Damon Nelson served his country nearly his entire life.
Before working for Rep. Devin Nunes and the people of California's 22nd Congressional District, Damon was proud to have served in the United States Air Force, where he met the love of his life and future wife, Brigadier General Dana Nelson.
He was a decorated veteran of the Persian gulf war, earning an Air Medal and three Air Achievement Medals.
Damon was known to be an immensely loyal colleague.
He worked in the office of former Intelligence Committee Chairman Devin Nunes for over 10 years.
Five years after his passing, Members and staff alike remember Damon for his hard work, boundless sense of humor, selfless commitment to mentoring others and service to the intelligence community and his country.
