Mrs. Augustine leaves behind an indelible legacy of love, leadership, and service to her community and family.
Born in 1940, in Detroit, Michigan, 
Mrs. Augustine demonstrated a hardworking and tenacious spirit from a young age.
After graduating from St. Mary's High School in Mount Clemens, Michigan, she worked as a receptionist and switchboard operator for the City.
Clements, where she met and married 
Mr. Len Augustine.
