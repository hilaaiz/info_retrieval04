The Secretary of State was asked— What assessment he has made of the potential impact of the autumn Budget 2024 on farming communities.
The Labour Government have committed £5 billion to the agricultural budget over the next two years—the biggest budget for sustainable food production and nature recovery in our history.
It is good for British farming, it is good for the country, and it should be welcomed by the Opposition.
This week, the president of the National Farmers Union of Scotland, Martin Kennedy, said: “The new UK Government’s first budget…hammered hard-working family farms and crofts with crippling tax bills”.
