Ms. Brunson's training in dance began at B.J.'s School of Dance in St. Petersburg, Florida, under the direction.
She continued to study dance at the Pinellas County School for the Arts at Gibbs High School and at St. Petersburg College, CoMotion Dance Theater.
Ms. Brunson received a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Dance from the University of Florida, New World School of the Arts in Miami, Florida, under the direction.
She also received a Master of Arts in Dance from the American University in Washington, D.C., under the direction.
