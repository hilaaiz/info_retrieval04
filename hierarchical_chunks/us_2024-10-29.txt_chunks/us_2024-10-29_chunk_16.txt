Mr. William Robertson Wainwright of Salisbury, Maryland as he approaches his 100th birthday on November 21st.
Born in 1924, 
Mr. Wainwright honorably served in the United States Army during World War II from 1943 until 1946.
It is a distinct privilege to recognize 
Mr. Wainwright on his upcoming 100th birthday and celebrate this wonderful milestone.
On behalf of Maryland's First Congressional District, I wish Mr. Wainwright a very happy birthday.
