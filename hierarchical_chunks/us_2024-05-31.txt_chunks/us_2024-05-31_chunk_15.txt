Mr. Johnson's career in the wine community spans over three decades, during which he has tirelessly worked to elevate the visibility and viability of the Wine Business Institute at Sonoma State University in Sonoma County, California.
Since assuming the role of Executive Director in 2010, 
Mr. Johnson has spearheaded initiatives that have propelled the department to new heights, generating millions of dollars in support for its students, faculty, and programs.
His visionary leadership and strategic acumen have been instrumental in shaping the future of wine education and business.
Beyond his professional career, 
Mr. Johnson's passion for wine runs deep.
While working at a French restaurant in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, he discovered his love for wine and embarked on a transformative career path that led him to California.
Mr. Johnson's journey from a tour guide at Christian Brothers to the helm of the Wine Business Institute is a testament to his unwavering determination and dedication to his craft.
What sets 
Mr. Johnson apart is not only his expertise in wine education and business but also his commitment to sharing his knowledge and passion with others.
Through his work at the Wine Business Institute, Ray has empowered countless individuals to pursue their passions and unlock their full potential in the wine community.
His belief in the transformative power of education is evident in every aspect of his work, from the classroom to the boardroom.
His dedication to education culminated in the launch of the Executive Wine MBA program in 2012, the first of its kind in the nation.
Additionally, 
Mr. Johnson played a pivotal role in founding the Sonoma State Wine Cellars, which provides students with invaluable hands-on experience in the wine community.
