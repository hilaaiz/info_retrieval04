Ms. GRANGER.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor NiKolas Rogers on being named the 2024 Youth of the Year at the Boys and Girls Clubs of Greater Tarrant County.
NiKolas has consistently demonstrated exceptional leadership, dedication, and commitment to excellence that truly sets him apart, making him a deserving recipient of this prestigious honor.
NiKolas has shown an unwavering passion for academic success and personal growth.
His involvement in Upward Bound has not only enriched his own life but also positively impacted those around him.
He has a unique ability to inspire his peers and lead by example, always striving to make our community a better place.
As a student at Trimble Tech High School, NiKolas excels, maintaining high grades and a strong commitment to his education.
He approaches every challenge with determination and a positive attitude, embodying the values of hard work and perseverance.
His intellectual curiosity and eagerness to learn are truly commendable.
NiKolas is also willing to lend a helping hand at any time and offer support to those in need.
His genuine concern for others and his ability to connect with people on a personal level make him a beloved member of Upward Bound and our community in Tarrant County.
