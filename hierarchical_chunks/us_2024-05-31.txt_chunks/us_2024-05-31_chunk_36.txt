Mr. MOSKOWITZ.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize 
Ms. Emilia McGovern, the first-place winner of the prestigious Pathfinder Scholarship in the Business category.
This scholarship is awarded to Palm Beach and Martin County students who have displayed excellence in their academic and communal endeavors in a multitude of categories.
Emilia's academic journey is a testament to her dedication and passion.
She earned an AICE Diploma with Distinction from Cambridge University and College Board Hispanic Recognition, showcasing her academic prowess.
Her leadership role in her high school's Marketing Academy and her commendable performance in DECA competitions, where she secured the 5th position in the state for Marketing Communications, demonstrate her commitment to her community.
Her aspiration to pursue a degree in public relations to become a social media manager is a reflection of her forward-thinking mindset and her desire to make a positive impact.
