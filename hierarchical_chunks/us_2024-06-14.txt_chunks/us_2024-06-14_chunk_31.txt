Mr. Lecik joined The Senior Health Insurance Program-SHIP in 2012 and mastered the Medicare program.
David's dedication to serving older adults, people with disabilities, and their caregivers across the State healthcare for Medicare and Medicaid beneficiaries has been at the forefront of his career.
He shared his knowledge and talents with the 1,000 SHIP counselors who work with both Medicare and Medicaid programs to assist eligible beneficiaries with lifesaving programs and benefits.
He re-wrote the training manual for SHIP and restructured charts and desk aids that summarized difficult subjects for optimal understanding.
His ability to explain the medical programs for seniors and the technical support he has given to hundreds of SHIP counselors is exceptional as he has championed high quality education and advocacy within in the SHIP program.
Mr. Lecik has not only supported Medicare recipients by providing counseling to ensure they retain and navigate their health care benefits but has also been a pillar of support for Senior Health Insurance Program (SHIP) counselors and professionals throughout the State by helping them navigate the complex Medicare and Medicaid systems.
He has provided countless older adults and professionals with the information needed to make informed decisions about their benefits and championed for the most vulnerable and limited income beneficiaries to ensure they have quality health coverage.
