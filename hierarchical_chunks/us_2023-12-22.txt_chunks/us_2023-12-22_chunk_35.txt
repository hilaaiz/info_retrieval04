His good works earned him accolades such as Santa Clara County Philanthropist of the Year, two Papal awards, and an Honorary Degree of Doctor of Public Service from Santa Clara University.
Mike Fox was deeply devoted to his family and his Catholic faith, and his commitment to both inspired his life and brought him great joy.
He was kind and affable and mentored many people over the years, ran marathons and loved Broadway musicals.
Any cause that would uplift others became his cause.
