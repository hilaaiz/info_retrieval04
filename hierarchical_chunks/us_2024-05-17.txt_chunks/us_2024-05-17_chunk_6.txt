For 170 years, Shiloh Old Site has been an advocate for justice, equality, and perseverance.
Throughout the years, this community has been a catalyst in halting the spread of racial injustice in the city education to Black residents to offset the shortfalls of a racially segregated educational system.
Through its ministries and its purposedriven youth mission, the church works to provide families and children with programming that nurtures a community service mindset.
Shiloh Old Site is a pillar of the Fredericksburg community--always ready to lend a helping hand to those in need.
The community's commitment to Fredericksburg and its residents is both inspiring and humbling.
