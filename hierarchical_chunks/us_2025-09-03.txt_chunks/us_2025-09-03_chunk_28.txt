Mr. Pieratt was born on August 16, 1946, and raised in a loving home.
He graduated from Lexington's Lafayette High School.
An outstanding athlete, he earned All-City, All-Central Kentucky, and All-State honors in football and attended Oklahoma State University on a football scholarship.
He was recognized for academics on the All-Big 8 Academic Team.
Following graduation, 
Mr. Pieratt returned to Lexington and married Patsy, his sweetheart who he met in seventh grade.
