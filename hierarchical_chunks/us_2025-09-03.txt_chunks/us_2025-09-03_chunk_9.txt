Mr. Murray's dedication to serving our communities has been incredible through his work to help develop Upstate New York and the North Country.
A lifelong New Yorker, 
Mr. Murray is from Peterboro, New York and currently resides in Lisbon, New York.
He earned a Bachelor of Arts in financial economics from the State University of New York at Potsdam and went on to work as a commercial lending officer for a local banking institution for over 15 years.
From 2002 to 2007, Murray worked as a USDA Rural Development loan specialist in the Canton Field Office before being named New York State Director for USDA Rural Development.
During his time as State Director, Murray spent a year on the USDA RD Community Economic Development team in Washington, D.C. and has served as the Associate Enterprise Director.
