Mr. Barajas is the founder of Motivational Design and has completed more than 500 public art projects throughout Southern California.
Some of his notable works include ``The Founders Mural,'' ``No Border Wall,'' ``Amor Si Se Puede,'' and ``Amigos Car Club,'' and many others.
He is the recipient of multiple honors, including a Southwestern College Honorary Degree, inclusion in the Mexican Heritage Foundation's 100 Portraits: Pioneers, Visionaries and Role Models, and recognition as a San Diego County Library Living Well Legend.
