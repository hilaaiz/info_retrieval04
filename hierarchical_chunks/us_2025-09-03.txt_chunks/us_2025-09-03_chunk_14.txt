Mr. Lucas began his career as a secondary mathematics teacher, where he created a classroom culture of rigor and relevance.
His students consistently demonstrated significant growth on state assessments, earning him recognition for narrowing achievement gaps and instilling academic discipline.
As Principal of Simmons High School, 
Mr. Lucas set a bold goal of earning a B rating.
Through systems that raised expectations, improved instruction, and increased student accountability, he led the school to measurable gains in academic performance, graduation rates, and climate.
In addition to school leadership, 
Mr. Lucas developed and expanded transformative academic programs, including: Dual Enrollment & Career Pathways with Mississippi Delta Community College and Tougaloo College.
ACT Prep Initiatives, including summer boot camps and classroom integration.
Credit Recovery & Summer Learning, led by certified teachers.
Middle College Readiness Programs for college and career acceleration.
Student Support Systems involving mentoring and behavior contracts.
