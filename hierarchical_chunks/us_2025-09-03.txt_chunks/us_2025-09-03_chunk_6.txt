Ms. STEFANIK.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor United States Air Force and Air National Guard Veteran Peter Tautznik.
Peter Tautznik, born on December 4, 1947, has dedicated his life to serving both his country and his community.
Peter served in the U.S. Air Force as a Vehicle Maintenance Superintendent, and later with the Air National Guard.
He achieved the rank of Senior Master Sergeant, reflecting not only his technical expertise but also his strong leadership abilities and commitment to his country.
During his military career, Peter earned an impressive number of commendations.
His honors include three Air Force Commendation Medals, two Air Force Achievement Medals, seven Air Force Longevity Service Awards, two National Defense Service Medals, and two Air Force Outstanding Unit Awards.
He was also awarded two Humane Service to New York State Medals and four Long and Faithful Service Awards, recognizing his ongoing commitment to excellence and selfless service.
Beyond his military service, Peter has been a dedicated and active member of his community.
For more than 35 years, he owned and operated PJ's Truck Repair, providing reliable service to his neighbors.
He has also served as a certified National Rifle Association (NRA) Instructor and as a Fulton County Sheriff's Licensing Course Instructor.
Today, Peter continues his service by maintaining the Lake Valley Ambulance Corp. vehicles, ensuring the readiness of critical emergency services in Fulton County.
On behalf of New York's 21st Congressional District, I am honored to recognize Senior Master Sergeant Peter Tautznik for his service, sacrifice, and unwavering dedication to both our Nation and our local community.
