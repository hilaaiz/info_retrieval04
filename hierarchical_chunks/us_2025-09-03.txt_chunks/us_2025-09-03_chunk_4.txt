Whereas, in 1925, the Claremont Graduate University was founded on the belief that higher education holds a vital role in shaping the future of our nation and in nurturing the intellectual and ethical foundations upon which our democracy thrives; and Whereas, for 100 years, the Claremont Graduate University has contributed immeasurably to the intellectual, cultural, and civic life of California, the nation, and the world through the education of generations of students, groundbreaking research, and public service; and Whereas, The Claremont Graduate University has long been a beacon of intellectual rigor, fostering a diverse and inclusive academic environment that has produced leaders and innovators in many fields.
The university's commitment to transdisciplinary education and research has had a profound impact on society; and Whereas, The alumni of the Claremont Graduate University are professionals from diverse cultures and backgrounds who have gone on to become members of Congress, university presidents, professors, health researchers, superintendents, classroom teachers, data analysts, deans, art directors, and community leaders; and Whereas, the 100th anniversary of the Claremont Graduate University is a moment to honor the legacy of its founders, celebrate a century of achievement, and inspire future generations to uphold the principles of academic inquiry, leadership, and service; Now, therefore, be it resolved that I, Congresswoman Judy Chu, representing California's 28th Congressional District, do hereby recognize and commend the Claremont Graduate University on the occasion of their 100th Anniversary, and extend sincere appreciation for their enduring contributions to the people of California and beyond.
