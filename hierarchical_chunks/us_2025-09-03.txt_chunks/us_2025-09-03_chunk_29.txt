Mr. Pieratt went to work at Pieratt's, the family business founded in 1946 by his father Frank, a WWII veteran.
The business started as a radio and small appliance repair shop and rapidly expanded.
Mr. Pieratt worked alongside his father, brothers, brother-in-law, and nephews and helped grow Pieratt's into one of the most respected retailers in Central Kentucky.
In 1988, Bruce Pieratt was named President and CEO, a role he held until 2021 when the company sold.
A true patriot who loved his country and believed in serving, Mr. Pieratt joined the Kentucky Army National Guard in 1968.
He had a long and distinguished military career over thirty-one years.
He served Kentucky as Assistant Adjutant General-Army and attained the rank of Brigadier General.
