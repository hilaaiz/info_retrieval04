A United Nations report recently confirmed that sexual violence was used against Israeli women on  7 October .
Last week I raised concerns with the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office that women did not feel that they could trust the UN enough to speak about their experiences.
Victims of sexual violence deserve a voice and to be treated with respect, so what can my right hon.
Friend do to encourage the international community to unite in calling out sexual violence and those who seek to deny that it happens?
