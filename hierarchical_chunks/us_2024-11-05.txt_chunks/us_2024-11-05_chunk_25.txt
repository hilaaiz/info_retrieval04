Mr. Bartee's contributions extend beyond his professional roles to his active involvement in the Vallejo Main Street Program, the Vallejo Chamber of Commerce, and the Downtown Central Core Restoration Corporation (CCRC), where he serves as Vice President.
He is also known for his work as a host and producer with VERBA and VCAT, providing a platform for local dialogue and community engagement.
Tom's commitment to community development, revitalization, and engagement has left an enduring mark in Vallejo.
