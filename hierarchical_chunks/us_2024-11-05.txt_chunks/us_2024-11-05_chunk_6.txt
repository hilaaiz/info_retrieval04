Mr. KENNEDY.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to celebrate 
Ms. Catherine O'Malley-Bastian on her 100th birthday.
As she celebrates her 100th birthday, her life story is a beautiful tapestry woven with love and resilience.
Born on December 13, 1924, 
Ms. O'Malley-Bastian has not only witnessed a century of history but has also shaped the lives of those around her with her unwavering spirit and dedication to her community.
