Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor and commemorate the birthday of United States Marine Corps Gunnery Sergeant John Basilone.

Basilone was a courageous and honorable Marine who was awarded both the Congressional Medal of Honor for his heroism during the Battle of Guadalcanal and the Navy Cross for his actions during the Battle.
His acts of heroism left him the only enlisted Marine to receive both decorations during World War II.
