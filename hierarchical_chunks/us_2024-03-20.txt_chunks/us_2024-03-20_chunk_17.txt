Ms. PETTERSEN.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Kelly Albrecht Throughout her distinguished 32-year career, Kelly has exemplified innovation in education.
Over her tenure, Kelly taught fifth grade and elementary music and took on leadership roles, including serving as Canon Exploratory School middle school principal.
After retiring, Kelly continued to shape the future of education in Fremont County as the Induction Coordinator and Teacher Development Coach for the Canon City School District.
Her creative and innovative style of leadership has touched the lives of countless students and new teachers starting on their professional journeys.
Kelly's legacy is a testament to her passion for education and her impact on creating a culture of learning and excellence within the community.
On behalf of the people of Colorado's Seventh Congressional District, it is my honor to congratulate Kelly Albrecht for her accomplishment and thank her for her contribution to our community.
