She was one of 315 sisters who voted to give up their vows and leave their lives as nuns after the opposition they faced from the Catholic Church.
It wasn't long after she left the order that 
Mrs. Rinaldi fell in love and married a widower, Neal Rinaldi, who had five children from his previous marriage.
Together, the couple moved to Banning, California where 
Mrs. Rinaldi was 
Mr. Rinaldi's caretaker until he passed away.
