HONORING JEFFREY BOOHER AS A DISTINGUISHED FIRST RESPONDER LEADER IN

CENTRAL FLORIDA

HON.
DARREN SOTO

Mr. SOTO.
Mr. Speaker, Jeffrey Booher is a certified firefighter/

lieutenant and paramedic with the City of Kissimmee Fire Department,

where he has served with distinction for over 20 years.
A native of

Central Florida, Jeffrey was inspired to enter the fire service

following the events of September 11, 2001.
Leaving behind a successful

career in construction management, he committed himself to public

service and began training at the Central Florida Fire Academy.
Throughout his tenure, Jeffrey has held numerous roles, including

firefighter/EMT, firefighter/paramedic, and driver/operator.
He

currently serves as a company officer, leading his crew with a strong

focus on safety, professionalism, and mentorship.
Jeffrey holds a bachelor's degree in fire administration from Waldorf

University, graduating with honors.
He has also completed specialized

training at the Emergency Services Leadership Institute and the

National Fire Academy in Emmitsburg, Maryland, with a focus on incident

command.
Jeffrey's technical credentials include technician-level

certifications in Rope Rescue, Vehicle Machinery Rescue, Confined Space

Rescue, and Trench Rescue.
His dedication has earned him multiple honors, including 2017 KFD

Officer of the Year, Citywide Employee of the Month in 2015 and 2024,

and City of Kissimmee Employee of the Year in 2024.
In 2019, he was

awarded the Honeywell DuPont Scholarship to attend FDIC International.
Beyond his operational duties, Jeffrey has contributed significantly

to department initiatives, serving on committees for firefighter

safety, apparatus and station design, and the hiring and training of

new firefighters.
He also serves as a trustee and co-chairman of the

Firefighter Pension Board.
His role as assistant accreditation manager

helped lead to the fire department's first international accreditation.
With unwavering dedication, technical expertise, and a heart for

service, Lieutenant Jeffrey Booher continues to embody the values of

leadership, integrity, and commitment that define the fire service.
