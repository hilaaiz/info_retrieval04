HON.

to pay a very special tribute to an outstanding high school athlete

from Ohio's Fifth Congressional District.
Ariannah Floyd, a senior at

Firelands High School, has represented her school ably on her way to

achieving the Division II State Title for the 300 Meter Hurdles at the

her school and community.

to Ariannah Floyd of Firelands High School.

the Fifth District of Ohio, I am proud to recognize this great

achievement.
