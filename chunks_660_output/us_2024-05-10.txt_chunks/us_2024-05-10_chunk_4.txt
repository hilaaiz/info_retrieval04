Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Keith Sorci for earning the Man of the Year Award from the Arvada Chamber of Commerce.
Keith has been serving his community for over 20 years in many capacities, including as president of the Arvada Gardeners, volunteering with the Arvada Historical Society and with local schools, and distributing food to those in need.
Keith also works with Jefferson County Justice Services which offers alternatives to incarceration for Coloradans, training others to be equally helpful in their work for the community.
His continued dedication to the Arvada Community showcases his spirit of service and this award acknowledges his hard work.
On behalf of the people of Colorado's Seventh Congressional District, it is my honor to congratulate Keith on achieving the 2023 Arvada Chamber of Commerce Man of the Year Award. ____________________

Date: 2024-05-10 Volume: 170, Issue: 82 Pages: E479 - E479 ================================================================================ [Extensions of Remarks] From the Congressional Record Online through the Government Publishing Office [ www.gpo.gov ] RECOGNIZING MARK McGOFF ______ of colorado in the house of representatives Ms. PETTERSEN.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Mark McGoff of Arvada, Colorado for winning the Pioneer Award from the Arvada Chamber of Commerce.
Mark has proudly served his community in Arvada for years as a member of the City Council, the Arvada Fire Board, and a volunteer for the Historical Society, Arvada Jefferson Kiwanis, and the Arvada Center.
He is an Emeritus Board Member of the Ralston House and is currently working to plan Arvada's anniversary celebration this year.
The Pioneer Award is granted when a previous winner of the Man or Woman of the Year Award continues to perform notable work for the people of Arvada.
Mark has done exactly that with his continual contributions to the community and longstanding dedication to public service.
On behalf of the people of Colorado's Seventh Congressional District, it is my honor to congratulate Mark McGoff for winning the Pioneer Award.
We thank him for his commitment to our community. ____________________

His willingness to go above and beyond in every task he undertakes exemplifies the true spirit of volunteerism and advocacy.

Whether it's rallying local businesses, community groups, or donors, Mike has a remarkable ability to inspire others to join our fight against hunger.
His strategic planning and effective communication skills have resulted in record- breaking donations, tremendous organizational growth, and increased awareness of food insecurity issues in our area.
Beyond his efforts in advocacy, Mike lends his talents to the organization's Fundraising Committee, ensuring each event is a success.
Mike always has a smile on his face and a genuine warmth in his heart, believing that we are all better together.
His positive attitude is infectious and genuine.
