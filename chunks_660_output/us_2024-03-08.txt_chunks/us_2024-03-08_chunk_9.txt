Date: 2024-03-08 Volume: 170, Issue: 42 Pages: E231 - E231 ================================================================================ [Extensions of Remarks] From the Congressional Record Online through the Government Publishing Office [ www.gpo.gov ] SPECIAL RECOGNITION OF ISABELLA PFEIL FOR WINNING THE DIVISION I ONE- METER DIVE STATE CHAMPIONSHIP ______ of ohio in the house of representatives Mr. LATTA.
Mr. Speaker, it is with a great deal of pride that I rise to pay a very special tribute to an outstanding high school diver in Ohio's Fifth Congressional District.
Isabella Pfeil, a senior at Avon Lake High School, has ably represented her school on her way to achieving her first Division I State Diving Title.
Isabella overcame the challenges posed by intense competition in her effort to win the Division I One-Meter Dive State Championship.
In pursuing the State Championship, Isabella defeated her opponent in the finals of the one-meter dive classification.
Her hard work and dedication in achieving this accomplishment is truly outstanding.
She has accomplished an impressive season record, bringing pride to her school and community.
Mr. Speaker, I ask my colleagues to join me in paying special tribute to Isabella Pfeil of Avon Lake High School.
On behalf of the people of the Fifth Congressional District of Ohio, I am proud to recognize this great achievement.
