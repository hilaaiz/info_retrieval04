Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor and

congratulate the Great Bridge High School Boys' Soccer team on winning

the Class 5 Region A Championship.
As their Congresswoman, I am proud of these young athletes and their

recent achievement.
Located in Chesapeake, Virginia, the Great Bridge High School

Wildcats were able to bring the Class 5 Region A Championship back home

to their fellow students, faculty, families, and friends through their

hard work and dedication to their team.
The Great Bridge Boys' Soccer

team is undefeated this season, and goalie, Griffin Thomas, recorded a

shutout in the net, and Carson Danner scored the game winning goal to

secure their championship.
As a former athlete myself, I want each of these young men to know

Virginia's 2nd District is extremely proud of their accomplishment and

applauds them on their well-earned victory.
Again, congratulations to

the Great Bridge High School Boys' Soccer team on winning the Class 5

Region A Championship.

RECOGNIZING THE GREENBRIER CHRISTIAN BASEBALL TEAM

_____

HON.
JENNIFER A. KIGGANS

Mrs. KIGGANS of Virginia.
Mr. Speaker, I rise today to honor and

congratulate the Greenbrier Christian Baseball team on winning the 2024

VISAA DII State Championship.
As their Congresswoman, I am proud of these young athletes and their

recent achievement.
Located in Chesapeake, Virginia, the Greenbrier Christian Gators were

able to bring the VISAA DII State Championship back home to their

fellow students, faculty, families, and friends by their hard work and

dedication to their team.
The Greenbrier Christian Baseball Team has

fifteen State Championship titles under their belt.
In the beginning of

the year this team was unranked and quickly became unstoppable

averaging 8.9 runs per game.
This reflects not only their athletic

capabilities, but also their steadfast determination to excel.
As a former athlete myself, I want each of these young men to know

Virginia's 2nd District is extremely proud of their accomplishment and

applauds them on their well-earned victory.
Again, congratulations to

the Greenbrier Christian Baseball Team on winning the 2024 VISAA DII

State Championship.
