Mary L. Adams has served with distinction as the Fifth District

Supervisor for the County of Monterey, skillfully navigating numerous

complex challenges with compassion and foresight.
Her accomplishments

exemplify a lifetime of hard work and community dedication.
Mary played a vital role in leading Monterey County through major

emergencies, including multiple atmospheric river events and wildfires

such as the Soberanes, River, Carmel, Dolan, and Palo Colorado fires.
Her leadership in organizing efforts, like the Big Sur Helicopter

Commodity Drop, provided critical support to isolated communities,

ensuring the safety and resilience of residents during these crises.
She has been instrumental in addressing key local challenges,

including coordination during the Pfeiffer Bridge repairs, implementing

safety measures along Highway 1 near Point Lobos State Park and the

Bixby Creek Bridge, and overseeing the Scenic Road Carmel Lagoon

Environmental Impact Report.
Each of these efforts has significantly

contributed to the preservation of Monterey County's natural beauty and

infrastructure.
Mary's leadership in infrastructure improvements has also been

transformative.
She spearheaded upgrades to Carmel Valley Road and

played a key role as Chair of the Carmel Valley Road Advisory

Committee, culminating in the finalization of the Carmel Valley Road

and Laureles Grade Roundabout project.
A steadfast advocate for housing solutions, Mary worked tirelessly to

update the Vacation Rental Ordinance, protect neighborhood character,

and champion sustainable, long-term housing initiatives.
Under her

leadership, the County invested in the Monterey Bay Economic

Partnership Housing Trust Fund, unlocking millions in funding for

affordable housing projects.
She also supported innovative public-

private partnerships and employer-sponsored housing initiatives, while

prioritizing the community's best interests in updating the Housing

Element.
During the unprecedented challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Mary

provided steady and compassionate leadership, ensuring critical

resources reached those in need.
Her advocacy extended to sustainable

water solutions, supporting the passage of Measure J for public water

ownership and hosting Regional Water Forums to engage the community on

pressing water challenges.
Throughout her tenure, Mary has exemplified transparency and

accountability in governance.
Her instrumental role in the hiring of

key county officials, including the County Administrative Officer and

County Counsel, has strengthened the foundation for Monterey County's

future success.
Mr. Speaker, I ask my colleagues to join me in celebrating the career

and accomplishments of Mary L. Adams.
Her legacy of leadership and

dedication to the people of Monterey County will inspire generations to

come.
We thank her for her service and wish her the very best in her

well-deserved retirement.

RECOGNIZING THE ENGAGEMENT OF BRIAN JOHNSON AND CAROLINE REYNOLDS

HON.
JACK BERGMAN

Mr. BERGMAN.
Mr. Speaker, it is my honor to congratulate Brian

Johnson and Caroline Reynolds on the occasion of their engagement.
It

is with great pleasure and excitement that I share the wonderful news.
Brian, a steadfast friend and dedicated professional, has been a true

partner in our work with Veterans Guardian, helping countless veterans

across the Nation achieve their well-deserved disability rating

increases.
Fate brought Brian and his incredible fiance, Caroline Reynolds,

together at the U.S. Open during an event hosted by our mutual friend

and colleague, Congressman Richard Hudson.
Their story reached a

beautiful milestone over the Thanksgiving holiday on November 26, when

Brian proposed to Caroline in her charming hometown of Nacogdoches,

Texas.
The engagement was a heartwarming celebration, surrounded by

family, friends, and joyous festivities.
Love and marriage represent the joining of two hearts and lives,

built on a foundation of trust, respect, and shared dreams.
Brian and

Caroline embody the promise to stand together through life's joys and

challenges, growing stronger as individuals and as partners.
Their love

story is a testament to the beauty of finding someone who not only

inspires you but also walks beside you every step of the way.
Mr. Speaker, let us all join in congratulating Brian and Caroline on

this exciting new chapter in their lives.
