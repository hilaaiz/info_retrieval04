Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, I rise today to recognize the contributions of Lisa Tinbrink Howard as she is honored as one of California's 8th Congressional District's Women of the Year.
Lisa is a leader who serves her community through her work, advocacy, and volunteer efforts.
Early in her career.
Lisa worked as an engineer, where she broke down barriers and was a trailblazer for other female engineers.
She later founded Tolenas Winery, which she operates with her husband in the Suisun Valley.
There they provide wine education and help others understand the invaluable contributions of local agriculture to our community.
Lisa has served on the Solano County Agricultural Advisory Committee and is Secretary of the Suisun Valley Vinters and Growers Association where she works with elected officials to advocate for farmers and winemakers.
Furthermore, Lisa serves her community through her volunteer work, which includes work with the local 4H, advocating for women in the wine and agricultural industries, and supporting military personnel.
She is also a proud and caring mother of three children and inspires others with her dedication to her faith.
family, business, advocacy, and community service.

it is my pleasure to honor and recognize Lisa Tinbrink Howard for her positive contributions to our community. ____________________

Date: 2023-10-12 Volume: 169, Issue: 167 Pages: E964 - E964 ================================================================================ [Extensions of Remarks] From the Congressional Record Online through the Government Publishing Office [ www.gpo.gov ] HONORING THE 2023 WOMEN OF THE YEAR: MARGARET KOLK ______ of california in the house of representatives Mr. GARAMENDI.
Mr. Speaker Pro Tempore, I rise today to recognize the contributions of Margaret Kolk as she is honored as one of California's 8th Congressional District's Women of the Year.
Maggie is a passionate public servant who demonstrates a tireless dedication to serving her community.
For the past nine years, Maggie has served as Chair of the Benicia Human Services Board, directing funding to local nonprofits that serve low-income Benicia residents and improving funding for city agencies.
She is also the Secretary of Sustainable Solano, an environmental organization championing renewable energy and sustainable food production services.
Along with her public service, Maggie has demonstrated equal dedication to serving her community through her volunteer work and political activism.
For the past three years, Maggie has served as manager of the Benicia Community Garden helping to maintain an important resource that brings local sustainable food to Benicia residents.
In 2016.
Maggie co-founded Progressive Democrats of Benicia, now the city's largest political organization, and is currently spearheading a local ballot initiative to raise revenues to fix Benicia roads.
She is known to never back down from the toughest challenges and to always offer her complete commitment to any undertaking.
On behalf of the people of California's 8th Congressional District, it is my pleasure to honor and recognize Margaret Kolk for her positive contributions to our community. ____________________
